--- title: Anna van der Berg subtitle: Miss Anna van der Berg is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Listry constituency in South Luzland. ---

Miss Van der Berg has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Miss Van der Berg has voted on 60 legislative proposals and spoke in 13 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2018
Internal Affairs Member 2018

Last election

Miss Van der Berg won the 2020 Luzland general election in Listry with 34.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 7.2% more than the second placed candidate.
