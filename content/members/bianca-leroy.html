--- title: Bianca Leroy subtitle: Dr Bianca Leroy is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Wolfsfurt constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Leroy has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Dr Leroy has voted on 62 legislative proposals and spoke in 43 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2018
Standards and Procedures Member 2018

Last election

Dr Leroy won the 2020 Luzland general election in Wolfsfurt with 31.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.8% more than the second placed candidate.
