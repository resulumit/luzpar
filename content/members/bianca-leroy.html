--- title: Bianca Leroy subtitle: Dr Bianca Leroy is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Wolfsfurt constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Leroy has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Dr Leroy has voted on 48 legislative proposals and spoke in 35 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2019
Standards and Procedures Member 2019

Last election

Dr Leroy won the 2020 Luzland general election in Wolfsfurt with 34.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 8.4% more than the second placed candidate.
