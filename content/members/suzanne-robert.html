--- title: Suzanne Robert subtitle: Dr Suzanne Robert is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Heusbeke constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Robert has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a trade union official.

Parliamentary work

Dr Robert has voted on 25 legislative proposals and spoke in 12 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2019
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2019

Last election

Dr Robert won the 2020 Luzland general election in Heusbeke with 29.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.2% more than the second placed candidate.
