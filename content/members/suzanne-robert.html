--- title: Suzanne Robert subtitle: Dr Suzanne Robert is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Heusbeke constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Robert has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a trade union official.

Parliamentary work

Dr Robert has voted on 42 legislative proposals and spoke in 29 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2018
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2018

Last election

Dr Robert won the 2020 Luzland general election in Heusbeke with 33.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.4% more than the second placed candidate.
