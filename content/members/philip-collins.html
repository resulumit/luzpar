--- title: Philip Collins subtitle: Dr Philip Collins is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Arford constituency in East Luzland. ---

Dr Collins has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a dentist.

Dr Collins has voted on 26 legislative proposals and spoke in 23 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.
