--- title: Philip Collins subtitle: Dr Philip Collins is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Arford constituency in East Luzland. ---

Dr Collins has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a dentist.

Parliamentary work

Dr Collins has voted on 48 legislative proposals and spoke in 43 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2012
External Affairs Member 2012

Last election

Dr Collins won the 2020 Luzland general election in Arford with 29% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.600000000000001% more than the second placed candidate.
