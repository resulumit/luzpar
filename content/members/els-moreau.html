--- title: Els Moreau subtitle: Dr Els Moreau is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Bredenisse constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Moreau has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a trade union official.

Parliamentary work

Dr Moreau has voted on 30 legislative proposals and spoke in 41 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2012
Standards and Procedures Member 2012

Last election

Dr Moreau won the 2020 Luzland general election in Bredenisse with 33.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.3% more than the second placed candidate.
