--- title: Els Moreau subtitle: Dr Els Moreau is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Bredenisse constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Moreau has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a trade union official.
