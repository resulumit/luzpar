--- title: Els Moreau subtitle: Dr Els Moreau is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Bredenisse constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Moreau has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a trade union official.

Parliamentary work

Dr Moreau has voted on 35 legislative proposals and spoke in 8 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2005
Standards and Procedures Member 2005

Last election

Dr Moreau won the 2020 Luzland general election in Bredenisse with 30.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.3% more than the second placed candidate.
