--- title: Els Moreau subtitle: Dr Els Moreau is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Bredenisse constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Moreau has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a trade union official.

Parliamentary work

Dr Moreau has voted on 61 legislative proposals and spoke in 30 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2019
Standards and Procedures Member 2019

Last election

Dr Moreau won the 2020 Luzland general election in Bredenisse with 28.7% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.6% more than the second placed candidate.
