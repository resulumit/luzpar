--- title: Alice Garcia subtitle: Ms Alice Garcia is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Genwijk constituency in West Luzland. ---

Ms Garcia has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a designer.

Parliamentary work

Ms Garcia has voted on 42 legislative proposals and spoke in 44 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2014
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2014

Last election

Ms Garcia won the 2020 Luzland general election in Genwijk with 32.7% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.2% more than the second placed candidate.
