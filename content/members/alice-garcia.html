--- title: Alice Garcia subtitle: Ms Alice Garcia is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Genwijk constituency in West Luzland. ---

Ms Garcia has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a designer.

Parliamentary work

Ms Garcia has voted on 45 legislative proposals and spoke in 7 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2017
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2017

Last election

Ms Garcia won the 2020 Luzland general election in Genwijk with 35.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 8.8% more than the second placed candidate.
