--- title: Helga Patel subtitle: Ms Helga Patel is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Haarlem constituency in South Luzland. ---

Ms Patel has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Ms Patel has voted on 34 legislative proposals and spoke in 30 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2012
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2012

Last election

Ms Patel won the 2020 Luzland general election in Haarlem with 29.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.7% more than the second placed candidate.
