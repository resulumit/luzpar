--- title: Helga Patel subtitle: Ms Helga Patel is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Haarlem constituency in South Luzland. ---

Ms Patel has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Ms Patel has voted on 53 legislative proposals and spoke in 42 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2016
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2016

Last election

Ms Patel won the 2020 Luzland general election in Haarlem with 28% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.8% more than the second placed candidate.
