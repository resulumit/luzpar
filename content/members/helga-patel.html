--- title: Helga Patel subtitle: Ms Helga Patel is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Haarlem constituency in South Luzland. ---

Ms Patel has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Ms Patel has voted on 23 legislative proposals and spoke in 7 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2020
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2020

Last election

Ms Patel won the 2020 Luzland general election in Haarlem with 30% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3% more than the second placed candidate.
