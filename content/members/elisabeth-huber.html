--- title: Elisabeth Huber subtitle: Miss Elisabeth Huber is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Rheidrisio constituency in North Luzland. ---

Miss Huber has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Miss Huber has voted on 27 legislative proposals and spoke in 45 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2015
External Affairs Member 2015

Last election

Miss Huber won the 2020 Luzland general election in Rheidrisio with 29.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2% more than the second placed candidate.
