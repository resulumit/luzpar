--- title: Elias Fischer subtitle: Dr Elias Fischer is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Gordola constituency in North Luzland. ---

Dr Fischer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a dentist.
