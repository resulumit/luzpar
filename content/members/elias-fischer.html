--- title: Elias Fischer subtitle: Dr Elias Fischer is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Gordola constituency in North Luzland. ---

Dr Fischer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a dentist.

Parliamentary work

Dr Fischer has voted on 31 legislative proposals and spoke in 35 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2004
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2004

Last election

Dr Fischer won the 2020 Luzland general election in Gordola with 32.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 8.2% more than the second placed candidate.
