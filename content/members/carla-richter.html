--- title: Carla Richter subtitle: Mrs Carla Richter is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Alenyonne constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mrs Richter has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Richter has voted on 36 legislative proposals and spoke in 19 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2020
Internal Affairs Member 2020

Last election

Mrs Richter won the 2020 Luzland general election in Alenyonne with 32.7% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.2% more than the second placed candidate.
