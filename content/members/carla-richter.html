--- title: Carla Richter subtitle: Mrs Carla Richter is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Alenyonne constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mrs Richter has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.
