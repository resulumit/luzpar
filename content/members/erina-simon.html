--- title: Erina Simon subtitle: Mrs Erina Simon is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Schärfelden constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mrs Simon has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a pharmacist.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Simon has voted on 27 legislative proposals and spoke in 39 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs First Vice Chair 2018
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2018

Last election

Mrs Simon won the 2020 Luzland general election in Schärfelden with 32.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 7.2% more than the second placed candidate.
