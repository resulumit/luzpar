--- title: Peter Sweet subtitle: Mr Peter Sweet is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Diessenstätten constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Sweet has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mr Sweet has voted on 37 legislative proposals and spoke in 21 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2019
Internal Affairs Member 2019

Last election

Mr Sweet won the 2020 Luzland general election in Diessenstätten with 31.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.7% more than the second placed candidate.
