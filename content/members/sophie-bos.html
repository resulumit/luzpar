--- title: Sophie Bos subtitle: Mrs Sophie Bos is a member of parliament (Independent), representing the Zotburg constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mrs Bos has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Bos has voted on 42 legislative proposals and spoke in 9 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2017
Constitutional Affairs Member 2017

Last election

Mrs Bos won the 2020 Luzland general election in Zotburg with 33.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.8% more than the second placed candidate.
