--- title: Sophie Bos subtitle: Mrs Sophie Bos is a member of parliament (Independent), representing the Zotburg constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mrs Bos has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Bos has voted on 46 legislative proposals and spoke in 24 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.
