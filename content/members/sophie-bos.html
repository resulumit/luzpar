--- title: Sophie Bos subtitle: Mrs Sophie Bos is a member of parliament (Independent), representing the Zotburg constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mrs Bos has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Bos has voted on 61 legislative proposals and spoke in 13 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2016
Constitutional Affairs Member 2016

Last election

Mrs Bos won the 2020 Luzland general election in Zotburg with 31.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5% more than the second placed candidate.
