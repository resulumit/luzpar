--- title: Helmut Mercier subtitle: Mr Helmut Mercier is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Clerroux constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Mercier has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a pilot.

Parliamentary work

Mr Mercier has voted on 49 legislative proposals and spoke in 36 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2018
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2018

Last election

Mr Mercier won the 2020 Luzland general election in Clerroux with 29.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.6% more than the second placed candidate.
