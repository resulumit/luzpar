--- title: Helmut Mercier subtitle: Mr Helmut Mercier is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Clerroux constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Mercier has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a pilot.
