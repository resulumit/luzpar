--- title: Salihu Lambert subtitle: Dr Salihu Lambert is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Angounnois constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Lambert has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an academic.

Parliamentary work

Dr Lambert has voted on 35 legislative proposals and spoke in 10 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Chair 2020
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2020

Last election

Dr Lambert won the 2020 Luzland general election in Angounnois with 32.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.1% more than the second placed candidate.
