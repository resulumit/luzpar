--- title: Salihu Lambert subtitle: Dr Salihu Lambert is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Angounnois constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Lambert has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an academic.

Parliamentary work

Dr Lambert has voted on 51 legislative proposals and spoke in 7 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Chair 2015
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2015

Last election

Dr Lambert won the 2020 Luzland general election in Angounnois with 31% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.2% more than the second placed candidate.
