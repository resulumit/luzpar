--- title: Julie Morel subtitle: Dr Julie Morel is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Kaisermold constituency in East Luzland. ---

Dr Morel has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.
