--- title: Julie Morel subtitle: Dr Julie Morel is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Kaisermold constituency in East Luzland. ---

Dr Morel has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Dr Morel has voted on 56 legislative proposals and spoke in 7 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Second Vice Chair 2020
Standards and Procedures Member 2020

Last election

Dr Morel won the 2020 Luzland general election in Kaisermold with 32% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.4% more than the second placed candidate.
