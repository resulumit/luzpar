--- title: Noah Ross subtitle: Dr Noah Ross is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Eisenling constituency in North Luzland. ---

Dr Ross has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a physician.

Parliamentary work

Dr Ross has voted on 44 legislative proposals and spoke in 35 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Second Vice Chair 2021
Standards and Procedures Member 2021

Last election

Dr Ross won the 2020 Luzland general election in Eisenling with 29.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.3% more than the second placed candidate.
