--- title: Noah Ross subtitle: Dr Noah Ross is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Eisenling constituency in North Luzland. ---

Dr Ross has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a physician.

Parliamentary work

Dr Ross has voted on 65 legislative proposals and spoke in 20 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Second Vice Chair 2011
Standards and Procedures Member 2011

Last election

Dr Ross won the 2020 Luzland general election in Eisenling with 30.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.6% more than the second placed candidate.
