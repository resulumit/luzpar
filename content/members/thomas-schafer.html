--- title: Thomas Schäfer subtitle: Mr Thomas Schäfer is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Heerenkum constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Schäfer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a pharmacist.

Parliamentary work

Mr Schäfer has voted on 55 legislative proposals and spoke in 8 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.
