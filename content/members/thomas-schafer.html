--- title: Thomas Schäfer subtitle: Mr Thomas Schäfer is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Heerenkum constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Schäfer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a pharmacist.

Parliamentary work

Mr Schäfer has voted on 25 legislative proposals and spoke in 40 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2015
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2015

Last election

Mr Schäfer won the 2020 Luzland general election in Heerenkum with 31% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.1% more than the second placed candidate.
