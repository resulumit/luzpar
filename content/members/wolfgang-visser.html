--- title: Wolfgang Visser subtitle: Mr Wolfgang Visser is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Gorstein constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mr Visser has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mr Visser has voted on 65 legislative proposals and spoke in 32 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2017
Constitutional Affairs Member 2017

Last election

Mr Visser won the 2020 Luzland general election in Gorstein with 33.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.9% more than the second placed candidate.
