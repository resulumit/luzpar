--- title: Wolfgang Visser subtitle: Mr Wolfgang Visser is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Gorstein constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mr Visser has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mr Visser has voted on 49 legislative proposals and spoke in 8 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2008
Constitutional Affairs Member 2008

Last election

Mr Visser won the 2020 Luzland general election in Gorstein with 27.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.1% more than the second placed candidate.
