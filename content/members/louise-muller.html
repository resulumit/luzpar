--- title: Louise Müller subtitle: Ms Louise Müller is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Coelen constituency in South Luzland. ---

Ms Müller has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Ms Müller has voted on 39 legislative proposals and spoke in 13 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2021
Internal Affairs Member 2021

Last election

Ms Müller won the 2020 Luzland general election in Coelen with 28.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.7% more than the second placed candidate.
