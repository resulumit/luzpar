--- title: Louise Müller subtitle: Ms Louise Müller is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Coelen constituency in South Luzland. ---

Ms Müller has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a judge.
