--- title: Louise Müller subtitle: Ms Louise Müller is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Coelen constituency in South Luzland. ---

Ms Müller has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Ms Müller has voted on 52 legislative proposals and spoke in 26 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2021
Internal Affairs Member 2021

Last election

Ms Müller won the 2020 Luzland general election in Coelen with 30.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.1% more than the second placed candidate.
