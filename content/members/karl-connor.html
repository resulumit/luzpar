--- title: Karl Connor subtitle: Mr Karl Connor is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Ebenhaus constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Connor has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a judge.
