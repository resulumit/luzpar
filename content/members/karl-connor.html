--- title: Karl Connor subtitle: Mr Karl Connor is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Ebenhaus constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Connor has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Mr Connor has voted on 24 legislative proposals and spoke in 13 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2010
Constitutional Affairs Member 2010

Last election

Mr Connor won the 2020 Luzland general election in Ebenhaus with 30.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.4% more than the second placed candidate.
