--- title: Emilia Bauer subtitle: Dr Emilia Bauer is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Kilnard constituency in East Luzland. ---

Dr Bauer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Dr Bauer has voted on 54 legislative proposals and spoke in 46 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs First Vice Chair 2011
Standards and Procedures Member 2011

Last election

Dr Bauer won the 2020 Luzland general election in Kilnard with 33.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.4% more than the second placed candidate.
