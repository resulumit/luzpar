--- title: Emilia Bauer subtitle: Dr Emilia Bauer is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Kilnard constituency in East Luzland. ---

Dr Bauer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Dr Bauer has voted on 24 legislative proposals and spoke in 7 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs First Vice Chair 2012
Standards and Procedures Member 2012

Last election

Dr Bauer won the 2020 Luzland general election in Kilnard with 28.5% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.1% more than the second placed candidate.
