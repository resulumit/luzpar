--- title: Walter Schneider subtitle: Mr Walter Schneider is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Besanmans constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Schneider has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Schneider has voted on 50 legislative proposals and spoke in 19 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2019
Constitutional Affairs Member 2019

Last election

Mr Schneider won the 2020 Luzland general election in Besanmans with 30.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.7% more than the second placed candidate.
