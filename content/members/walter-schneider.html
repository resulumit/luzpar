--- title: Walter Schneider subtitle: Mr Walter Schneider is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Besanmans constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Schneider has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Schneider has voted on 27 legislative proposals and spoke in 38 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2014
Constitutional Affairs Member 2014

Last election

Mr Schneider won the 2020 Luzland general election in Besanmans with 34.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 7.5% more than the second placed candidate.
