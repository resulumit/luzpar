--- title: Andreas Tucker subtitle: Mr Andreas Tucker is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Niedernholz constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Tucker has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Tucker has voted on 36 legislative proposals and spoke in 13 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2013
Internal Affairs Member 2013

Last election

Mr Tucker won the 2020 Luzland general election in Niedernholz with 32.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.5% more than the second placed candidate.
