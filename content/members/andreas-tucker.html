--- title: Andreas Tucker subtitle: Mr Andreas Tucker is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Niedernholz constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Tucker has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Tucker has voted on 57 legislative proposals and spoke in 45 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2020
Internal Affairs Member 2020

Last election

Mr Tucker won the 2020 Luzland general election in Niedernholz with 29.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.2% more than the second placed candidate.
