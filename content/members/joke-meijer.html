--- title: Joke Meijer subtitle: Mr Joke Meijer is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Hartling constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Meijer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mr Meijer has voted on 56 legislative proposals and spoke in 37 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2016
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2016

Last election

Mr Meijer won the 2020 Luzland general election in Hartling with 28.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.7% more than the second placed candidate.
