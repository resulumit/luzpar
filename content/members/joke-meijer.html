--- title: Joke Meijer subtitle: Mr Joke Meijer is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Hartling constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Meijer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mr Meijer has voted on 52 legislative proposals and spoke in 14 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2019
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2019

Last election

Mr Meijer won the 2020 Luzland general election in Hartling with 31.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.8% more than the second placed candidate.
