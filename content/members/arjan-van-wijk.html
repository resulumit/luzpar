--- title: Arjan van Wijk subtitle: Mr Arjan van Wijk is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Dorschoten constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Van Wijk has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Mr Van Wijk has voted on 60 legislative proposals and spoke in 41 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2016
Constitutional Affairs Member 2016

Last election

Mr Van Wijk won the 2020 Luzland general election in Dorschoten with 31.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5% more than the second placed candidate.

Party Votes Share
Conservative 24,087 31.6%
Labour 20,233 26.6%
