--- title: Arjan van Wijk subtitle: Mr Arjan van Wijk is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Dorschoten constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Van Wijk has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Mr Van Wijk has voted on 52 legislative proposals and spoke in 22 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.
