--- title: Ambre Meier subtitle: Mr Ambre Meier is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Abenfeld constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Meier has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a designer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Meier has voted on 34 legislative proposals and spoke in 9 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.
