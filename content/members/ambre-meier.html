--- title: Ambre Meier subtitle: Mr Ambre Meier is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Abenfeld constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Meier has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a designer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Meier has voted on 23 legislative proposals and spoke in 20 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2006
External Affairs Member 2006

Last election

Mr Meier won the 2020 Luzland general election in Abenfeld with 28.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.8% more than the second placed candidate.
