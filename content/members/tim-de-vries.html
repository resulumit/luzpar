--- title: Tim de Vries subtitle: Mr Tim de Vries is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Yougdare constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mr De Vries has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mr De Vries has voted on 50 legislative proposals and spoke in 10 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Second Vice Chair 2016
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2016

Last election

Mr De Vries won the 2020 Luzland general election in Yougdare with 30.7% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.3% more than the second placed candidate.

Party Votes Share
Green 23,613 30.7%
