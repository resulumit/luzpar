--- title: Tim de Vries subtitle: Mr Tim de Vries is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Yougdare constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mr De Vries has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mr De Vries has voted on 45 legislative proposals and spoke in 14 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Second Vice Chair 2019
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2019

Last election

Mr De Vries won the 2020 Luzland general election in Yougdare with 29.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.3% more than the second placed candidate.
