--- title: Lara Groen subtitle: Ms Lara Groen is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Loughnasloe constituency in South Luzland. ---

Ms Groen has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a trade union official.

Parliamentary work

Ms Groen has voted on 48 legislative proposals and spoke in 14 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2019
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2019

Last election

Ms Groen won the 2020 Luzland general election in Loughnasloe with 32.5% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.2% more than the second placed candidate.
