--- title: Lara Groen subtitle: Ms Lara Groen is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Loughnasloe constituency in South Luzland. ---

Ms Groen has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a trade union official.

Parliamentary work

Ms Groen has voted on 44 legislative proposals and spoke in 34 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2020
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2020

Last election

Ms Groen won the 2020 Luzland general election in Loughnasloe with 35.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 7.8% more than the second placed candidate.
