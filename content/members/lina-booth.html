--- title: Lina Booth subtitle: Miss Lina Booth is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Leonrau constituency in North Luzland. ---

Miss Booth has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Miss Booth has voted on 31 legislative proposals and spoke in 38 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2013
Internal Affairs Member 2013

Last election

Miss Booth won the 2020 Luzland general election in Leonrau with 27.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.8% more than the second placed candidate.
