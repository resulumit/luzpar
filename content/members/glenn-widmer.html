--- title: Glenn Widmer subtitle: Ms Glenn Widmer is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Galbridge constituency in East Luzland. ---

Ms Widmer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Ms Widmer has voted on 42 legislative proposals and spoke in 21 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2020
External Affairs Member 2020

Last election

Ms Widmer won the 2020 Luzland general election in Galbridge with 29.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.2% more than the second placed candidate.
