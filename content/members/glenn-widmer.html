--- title: Glenn Widmer subtitle: Ms Glenn Widmer is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Galbridge constituency in East Luzland. ---

Ms Widmer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Ms Widmer has voted on 30 legislative proposals and spoke in 46 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.
