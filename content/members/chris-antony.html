--- title: Chris Antony subtitle: Mr Chris Antony is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Benwerder constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Antony has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a journalist.

Parliamentary work

Mr Antony has voted on 28 legislative proposals and spoke in 21 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2021
External Affairs Member 2021

Last election

Mr Antony won the 2020 Luzland general election in Benwerder with 30.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.5% more than the second placed candidate.
