--- title: Josef Dubois subtitle: Mr Josef Dubois is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Lieszona constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Dubois has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Dubois has voted on 37 legislative proposals and spoke in 29 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Second Vice Chair 2004
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2004

Last election

Mr Dubois won the 2020 Luzland general election in Lieszona with 33.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 7.4% more than the second placed candidate.
