--- title: Josef Dubois subtitle: Mr Josef Dubois is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Lieszona constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Dubois has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Dubois has voted on 26 legislative proposals and spoke in 32 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Second Vice Chair 2021
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2021

Last election

Mr Dubois won the 2020 Luzland general election in Lieszona with 31.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.7% more than the second placed candidate.
