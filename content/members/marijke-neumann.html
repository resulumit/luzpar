--- title: Marijke Neumann subtitle: Dr Marijke Neumann is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Diessenzona constituency in East Luzland. ---

Dr Neumann has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.
