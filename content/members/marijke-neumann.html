--- title: Marijke Neumann subtitle: Dr Marijke Neumann is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Diessenzona constituency in East Luzland. ---

Dr Neumann has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Dr Neumann has voted on 40 legislative proposals and spoke in 33 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2010
Internal Affairs Member 2010

Last election

Dr Neumann won the 2020 Luzland general election in Diessenzona with 28.7% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.1% more than the second placed candidate.
