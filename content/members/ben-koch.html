--- title: Ben Koch subtitle: Mr Ben Koch is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Illson constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Koch has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Koch has voted on 35 legislative proposals and spoke in 41 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2018
External Affairs Member 2018

Last election

Mr Koch won the 2020 Luzland general election in Illson with 32.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.9% more than the second placed candidate.
