--- title: Samuel Mulder subtitle: Mr Samuel Mulder is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Romkastel constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Mulder has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a journalist.

Parliamentary work

Mr Mulder has voted on 62 legislative proposals and spoke in 38 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2021
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2021

Last election

Mr Mulder won the 2020 Luzland general election in Romkastel with 29.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.8% more than the second placed candidate.
