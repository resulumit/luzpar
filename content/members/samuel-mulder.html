--- title: Samuel Mulder subtitle: Mr Samuel Mulder is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Romkastel constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Mulder has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a journalist.

Parliamentary work

Mr Mulder has voted on 38 legislative proposals and spoke in 10 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2016
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2016

Last election

Mr Mulder won the 2020 Luzland general election in Romkastel with 29.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.600000000000001% more than the second placed candidate.
