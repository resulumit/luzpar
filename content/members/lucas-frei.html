--- title: Lucas Frei subtitle: Mr Lucas Frei is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Amdeck constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mr Frei has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Frei has voted on 44 legislative proposals and spoke in 15 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Second Vice Chair 2015
Standards and Procedures Member 2015

Last election

Mr Frei won the 2020 Luzland general election in Amdeck with 29.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.2% more than the second placed candidate.
