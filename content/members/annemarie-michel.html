--- title: Annemarie Michel subtitle: Miss Annemarie Michel is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Weißenbeck constituency in South Luzland. ---

Miss Michel has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an architect.

Parliamentary work

Miss Michel has voted on 23 legislative proposals and spoke in 7 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2016
Constitutional Affairs Member 2016

Last election

Miss Michel won the 2020 Luzland general election in Weißenbeck with 32.7% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.6% more than the second placed candidate.
