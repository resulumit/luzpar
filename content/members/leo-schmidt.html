--- title: Léo Schmidt subtitle: Mr Léo Schmidt is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Gladenheim constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Schmidt has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a pilot.

Parliamentary work

Mr Schmidt has voted on 39 legislative proposals and spoke in 30 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2011
Standards and Procedures Member 2011

Last election

Mr Schmidt won the 2020 Luzland general election in Gladenheim with 29.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.9% more than the second placed candidate.
