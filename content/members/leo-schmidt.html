--- title: Léo Schmidt subtitle: Mr Léo Schmidt is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Gladenheim constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Schmidt has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a pilot.

Parliamentary work

Mr Schmidt has voted on 65 legislative proposals and spoke in 38 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2020
Standards and Procedures Member 2020

Last election

Mr Schmidt won the 2020 Luzland general election in Gladenheim with 29.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.4% more than the second placed candidate.
