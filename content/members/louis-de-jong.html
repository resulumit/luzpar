--- title: Louis de Jong subtitle: Mr Louis de Jong is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Épiluçon constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr De Jong has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Mr De Jong has voted on 56 legislative proposals and spoke in 7 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2015
Constitutional Affairs Member 2015

Last election

Mr De Jong won the 2020 Luzland general election in Épiluçon with 29.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.8% more than the second placed candidate.
