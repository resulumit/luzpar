--- title: Louis de Jong subtitle: Mr Louis de Jong is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Épiluçon constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr De Jong has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an engineer.
