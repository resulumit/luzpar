--- title: Evelyn Vink subtitle: Mrs Evelyn Vink is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Culemdam constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mrs Vink has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Vink has voted on 34 legislative proposals and spoke in 46 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs First Vice Chair 2015
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2015

Last election

Mrs Vink won the 2020 Luzland general election in Culemdam with 29.5% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.2% more than the second placed candidate.
