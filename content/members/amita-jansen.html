--- title: Amita Jansen subtitle: Dr Amita Jansen is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Ebergeismar constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Jansen has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Dr Jansen has voted on 58 legislative proposals and spoke in 39 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Chair 2020
Internal Affairs Member 2020

Last election

Dr Jansen won the 2020 Luzland general election in Ebergeismar with 31.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.6% more than the second placed candidate.
