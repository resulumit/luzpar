--- title: Amita Jansen subtitle: Dr Amita Jansen is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Ebergeismar constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Jansen has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.
