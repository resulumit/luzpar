--- title: David Hoffmann subtitle: Mr David Hoffmann is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Roesberg constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Hoffmann has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Mr Hoffmann has voted on 42 legislative proposals and spoke in 11 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.
