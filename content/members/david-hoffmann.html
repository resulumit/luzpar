--- title: David Hoffmann subtitle: Mr David Hoffmann is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Roesberg constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Hoffmann has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Hoffmann has voted on 55 legislative proposals and spoke in 31 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2011
External Affairs Member 2011

Last election

Mr Hoffmann won the 2020 Luzland general election in Roesberg with 27.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2% more than the second placed candidate.
