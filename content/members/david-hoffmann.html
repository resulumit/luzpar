--- title: David Hoffmann subtitle: Mr David Hoffmann is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Roesberg constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Hoffmann has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Hoffmann has voted on 52 legislative proposals and spoke in 42 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2020
External Affairs Member 2020

Last election

Mr Hoffmann won the 2020 Luzland general election in Roesberg with 26.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.5% more than the second placed candidate.
