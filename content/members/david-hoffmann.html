--- title: David Hoffmann subtitle: Mr David Hoffmann is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Roesberg constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Hoffmann has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Hoffmann has voted on 36 legislative proposals and spoke in 17 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2021
External Affairs Member 2021

Last election

Mr Hoffmann won the 2020 Luzland general election in Roesberg with 28.5% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.3% more than the second placed candidate.
