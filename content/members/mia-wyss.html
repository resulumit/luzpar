--- title: Mia Wyss subtitle: Ms Mia Wyss is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Leonhausen constituency in North Luzland. ---

Ms Wyss has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a designer.

Parliamentary work

Ms Wyss has voted on 57 legislative proposals and spoke in 31 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.
