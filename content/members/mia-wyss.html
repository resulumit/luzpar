--- title: Mia Wyss subtitle: Ms Mia Wyss is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Leonhausen constituency in North Luzland. ---

Ms Wyss has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a designer.

Parliamentary work

Ms Wyss has voted on 47 legislative proposals and spoke in 25 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2021
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2021

Last election

Ms Wyss won the 2020 Luzland general election in Leonhausen with 34.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 7.6% more than the second placed candidate.
