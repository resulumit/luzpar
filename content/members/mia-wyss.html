--- title: Mia Wyss subtitle: Ms Mia Wyss is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Leonhausen constituency in North Luzland. ---

Ms Wyss has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a designer.

Parliamentary work

Ms Wyss has voted on 35 legislative proposals and spoke in 31 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2010
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2010

Last election

Ms Wyss won the 2020 Luzland general election in Leonhausen with 28.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.4% more than the second placed candidate.
