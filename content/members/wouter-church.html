--- title: Wouter Church subtitle: Mr Wouter Church is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Shanmeath constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Church has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an architect.

Parliamentary work

Mr Church has voted on 42 legislative proposals and spoke in 39 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2020
External Affairs Member 2020

Last election

Mr Church won the 2020 Luzland general election in Shanmeath with 32.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 7.4% more than the second placed candidate.
