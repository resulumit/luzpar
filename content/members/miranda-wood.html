--- title: Miranda Wood subtitle: Dr Miranda Wood is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Zoteinde constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Wood has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a journalist.

Parliamentary work

Dr Wood has voted on 65 legislative proposals and spoke in 37 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2013
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2013

Last election

Dr Wood won the 2020 Luzland general election in Zoteinde with 32.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.4% more than the second placed candidate.
