--- title: Jules Brunner subtitle: Dr Jules Brunner is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Ligny constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Brunner has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an academic.
