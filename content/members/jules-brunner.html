--- title: Jules Brunner subtitle: Dr Jules Brunner is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Ligny constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Brunner has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an academic.

Parliamentary work

Dr Brunner has voted on 54 legislative proposals and spoke in 43 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2018
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2018

Last election

Dr Brunner won the 2020 Luzland general election in Ligny with 33.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.7% more than the second placed candidate.
