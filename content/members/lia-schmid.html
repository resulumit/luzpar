--- title: Lia Schmid subtitle: Dr Lia Schmid is a member of parliament (Independent), representing the Menkkon constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Schmid has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Dr Schmid has voted on 58 legislative proposals and spoke in 44 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights First Vice Chair 2021
External Affairs Member 2021

Last election

Dr Schmid won the 2020 Luzland general election in Menkkon with 34.5% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.6% more than the second placed candidate.
