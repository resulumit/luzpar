--- title: Lia Schmid subtitle: Dr Lia Schmid is a member of parliament (Independent), representing the Menkkon constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Schmid has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Dr Schmid has voted on 61 legislative proposals and spoke in 37 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.
