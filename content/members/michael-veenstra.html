--- title: Michael Veenstra subtitle: Dr Michael Veenstra is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Tangerhöring constituency in North Luzland. ---

Dr Veenstra has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an academic.

Parliamentary work

Dr Veenstra has voted on 60 legislative proposals and spoke in 9 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2021
Standards and Procedures Member 2021

Last election

Dr Veenstra won the 2020 Luzland general election in Tangerhöring with 30.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.7% more than the second placed candidate.
