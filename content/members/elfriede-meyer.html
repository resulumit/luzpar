--- title: Elfriede Meyer subtitle: Dr Elfriede Meyer is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Balnon constituency in East Luzland. ---

Dr Meyer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Dr Meyer has voted on 62 legislative proposals and spoke in 42 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2007
External Affairs Member 2007

Last election

Dr Meyer won the 2020 Luzland general election in Balnon with 28.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.8% more than the second placed candidate.
