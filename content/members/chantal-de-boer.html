--- title: Chantal de Boer subtitle: Ms Chantal de Boer is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Riviervik constituency in South Luzland. ---

Ms De Boer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Ms De Boer has voted on 58 legislative proposals and spoke in 13 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2015
Constitutional Affairs Member 2015

Last election

Ms De Boer won the 2020 Luzland general election in Riviervik with 28.7% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.4% more than the second placed candidate.
