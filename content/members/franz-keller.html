--- title: Franz Keller subtitle: Mr Franz Keller is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Amrisdris constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Keller has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Mr Keller has voted on 52 legislative proposals and spoke in 45 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Chair 2017
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2017

Last election

Mr Keller won the 2020 Luzland general election in Amrisdris with 31% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.3% more than the second placed candidate.
