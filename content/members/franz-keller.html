--- title: Franz Keller subtitle: Mr Franz Keller is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Amrisdris constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Keller has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Mr Keller has voted on 48 legislative proposals and spoke in 20 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Chair 2020
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2020

Last election

Mr Keller won the 2020 Luzland general election in Amrisdris with 29.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.2% more than the second placed candidate.
