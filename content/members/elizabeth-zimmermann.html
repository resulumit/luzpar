--- title: Elizabeth Zimmermann subtitle: Mrs Elizabeth Zimmermann is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Innsling constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mrs Zimmermann has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Zimmermann has voted on 40 legislative proposals and spoke in 35 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2015
Standards and Procedures Member 2015

Last election

Mrs Zimmermann won the 2020 Luzland general election in Innsling with 29.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.8% more than the second placed candidate.
