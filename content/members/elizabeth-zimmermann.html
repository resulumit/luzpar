--- title: Elizabeth Zimmermann subtitle: Mrs Elizabeth Zimmermann is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Innsling constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mrs Zimmermann has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a surgent.
