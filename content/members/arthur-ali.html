--- title: Arthur Ali subtitle: Mr Arthur Ali is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Mühlshafen constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mr Ali has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an architect.

Parliamentary work

Mr Ali has voted on 53 legislative proposals and spoke in 43 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2021
Standards and Procedures Member 2021

Last election

Mr Ali won the 2020 Luzland general election in Mühlshafen with 27.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.1% more than the second placed candidate.
