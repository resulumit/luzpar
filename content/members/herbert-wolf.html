--- title: Herbert Wolf subtitle: Mr Herbert Wolf is a member of parliament (Independent), representing the Waidzell constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Wolf has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mr Wolf has voted on 30 legislative proposals and spoke in 8 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2020
External Affairs Member 2020

Last election

Mr Wolf won the 2020 Luzland general election in Waidzell with 26.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.599999999999998% more than the second placed candidate.
