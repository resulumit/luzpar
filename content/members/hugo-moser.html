--- title: Hugo Moser subtitle: Mr Hugo Moser is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Rivierren constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Moser has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Moser has voted on 59 legislative proposals and spoke in 27 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2016
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2016

Last election

Mr Moser won the 2020 Luzland general election in Rivierren with 28.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.4% more than the second placed candidate.
