--- title: Hugo Moser subtitle: Mr Hugo Moser is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Rivierren constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Moser has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Moser has voted on 59 legislative proposals and spoke in 7 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2021
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2021

Last election

Mr Moser won the 2020 Luzland general election in Rivierren with 30.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.5% more than the second placed candidate.
