--- title: Hugo Moser subtitle: Mr Hugo Moser is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Rivierren constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Moser has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an engineer.
