--- title: Gerhard Smit subtitle: Mr Gerhard Smit is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Roosenzee constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Smit has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an architect.

Parliamentary work

Mr Smit has voted on 48 legislative proposals and spoke in 23 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2011
Standards and Procedures Member 2011

Last election

Mr Smit won the 2020 Luzland general election in Roosenzee with 33.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 8.3% more than the second placed candidate.
