--- title: Gerhard Smit subtitle: Mr Gerhard Smit is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Roosenzee constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Smit has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an architect.

Parliamentary work

Mr Smit has voted on 64 legislative proposals and spoke in 9 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2021
Standards and Procedures Member 2021

Last election

Mr Smit won the 2020 Luzland general election in Roosenzee with 31.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.6% more than the second placed candidate.
