--- title: Sofia Girard subtitle: Miss Sofia Girard is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Tongschot constituency in North Luzland. ---

Miss Girard has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a trade union official.

Parliamentary work

Miss Girard has voted on 61 legislative proposals and spoke in 46 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2012
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2012

Last election

Miss Girard won the 2020 Luzland general election in Tongschot with 31.7% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.1% more than the second placed candidate.
