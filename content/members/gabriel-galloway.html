--- title: Gabriel Galloway subtitle: Mrs Gabriel Galloway is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Eastlarney constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mrs Galloway has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Galloway has voted on 29 legislative proposals and spoke in 44 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2020
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2020

Last election

Mrs Galloway won the 2020 Luzland general election in Eastlarney with 30.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4% more than the second placed candidate.
