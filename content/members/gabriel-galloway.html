--- title: Gabriel Galloway subtitle: Mrs Gabriel Galloway is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Eastlarney constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mrs Galloway has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Galloway has voted on 61 legislative proposals and spoke in 14 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2004
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2004

Last election

Mrs Galloway won the 2020 Luzland general election in Eastlarney with 32.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.5% more than the second placed candidate.
