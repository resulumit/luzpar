--- title: Gabriel Galloway subtitle: Mrs Gabriel Galloway is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Eastlarney constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mrs Galloway has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Galloway has voted on 23 legislative proposals and spoke in 37 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2011
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2011

Last election

Mrs Galloway won the 2020 Luzland general election in Eastlarney with 31.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.2% more than the second placed candidate.
