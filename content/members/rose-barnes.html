--- title: Rose Barnes subtitle: Dr Rose Barnes is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Dillon constituency in North Luzland. ---

Dr Barnes has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Dr Barnes has voted on 36 legislative proposals and spoke in 33 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2015
External Affairs Member 2015

Last election

Dr Barnes won the 2020 Luzland general election in Dillon with 30.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3% more than the second placed candidate.
