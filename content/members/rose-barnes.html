--- title: Rose Barnes subtitle: Dr Rose Barnes is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Dillon constituency in North Luzland. ---

Dr Barnes has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Dr Barnes has voted on 58 legislative proposals and spoke in 9 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2017
External Affairs Member 2017

Last election

Dr Barnes won the 2020 Luzland general election in Dillon with 28.5% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.5% more than the second placed candidate.
