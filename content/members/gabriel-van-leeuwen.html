--- title: Gabriel van Leeuwen subtitle: Dr Gabriel van Leeuwen is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Porrenbourg constituency in North Luzland. ---

Dr Van Leeuwen has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an academic.

Parliamentary work

Dr Van Leeuwen has voted on 50 legislative proposals and spoke in 20 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2013
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2013

Last election

Dr Van Leeuwen won the 2020 Luzland general election in Porrenbourg with 32.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.4% more than the second placed candidate.
