--- title: Amanda Martin subtitle: Mrs Amanda Martin is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Bunlarney constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mrs Martin has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Martin has voted on 40 legislative proposals and spoke in 17 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2010
Internal Affairs Member 2010

Last election

Mrs Martin won the 2020 Luzland general election in Bunlarney with 33.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.4% more than the second placed candidate.
