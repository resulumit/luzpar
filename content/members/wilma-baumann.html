--- title: Wilma Baumann subtitle: Mrs Wilma Baumann is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Granderry constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mrs Baumann has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Baumann has voted on 37 legislative proposals and spoke in 9 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Chair 2020
Standards and Procedures Member 2020

Last election

Mrs Baumann won the 2020 Luzland general election in Granderry with 27% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.9% more than the second placed candidate.
