--- title: Wilma Baumann subtitle: Mrs Wilma Baumann is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Granderry constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mrs Baumann has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Baumann has voted on 56 legislative proposals and spoke in 13 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.
