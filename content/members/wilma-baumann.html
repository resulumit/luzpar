--- title: Wilma Baumann subtitle: Mrs Wilma Baumann is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Granderry constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mrs Baumann has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Baumann has voted on 63 legislative proposals and spoke in 36 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Chair 2021
Standards and Procedures Member 2021

Last election

Mrs Baumann won the 2020 Luzland general election in Granderry with 33.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 7.3% more than the second placed candidate.
