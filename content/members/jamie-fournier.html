--- title: Jamie Fournier subtitle: Mr Jamie Fournier is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Borgny constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Fournier has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Mr Fournier has voted on 64 legislative proposals and spoke in 31 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Chair 2020
Standards and Procedures Member 2020

Last election

Mr Fournier won the 2020 Luzland general election in Borgny with 29.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.5% more than the second placed candidate.
