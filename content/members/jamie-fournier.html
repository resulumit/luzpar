--- title: Jamie Fournier subtitle: Mr Jamie Fournier is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Borgny constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Fournier has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Mr Fournier has voted on 30 legislative proposals and spoke in 43 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Chair 2012
Standards and Procedures Member 2012

Last election

Mr Fournier won the 2020 Luzland general election in Borgny with 30.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.8% more than the second placed candidate.
