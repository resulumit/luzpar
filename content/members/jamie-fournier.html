--- title: Jamie Fournier subtitle: Mr Jamie Fournier is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Borgny constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Fournier has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Mr Fournier has voted on 61 legislative proposals and spoke in 34 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Chair 2017
Standards and Procedures Member 2017

Last election

Mr Fournier won the 2020 Luzland general election in Borgny with 27.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.800000000000001% more than the second placed candidate.
