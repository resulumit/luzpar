--- title: Chloë Bakker subtitle: Miss Chloë Bakker is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Steffisfelden constituency in South Luzland. ---

Miss Bakker has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Miss Bakker has voted on 40 legislative proposals and spoke in 23 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.
