--- title: Chloë Bakker subtitle: Miss Chloë Bakker is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Steffisfelden constituency in South Luzland. ---

Miss Bakker has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Miss Bakker has voted on 29 legislative proposals and spoke in 43 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2005
Constitutional Affairs Member 2005

Last election

Miss Bakker won the 2020 Luzland general election in Steffisfelden with 34.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.5% more than the second placed candidate.
