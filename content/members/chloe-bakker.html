--- title: Chloë Bakker subtitle: Miss Chloë Bakker is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Steffisfelden constituency in South Luzland. ---

Miss Bakker has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Miss Bakker has voted on 44 legislative proposals and spoke in 45 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2021
Constitutional Affairs Member 2021
