--- title: Chloë Bakker subtitle: Miss Chloë Bakker is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Steffisfelden constituency in South Luzland. ---

Miss Bakker has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Miss Bakker has voted on 62 legislative proposals and spoke in 11 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2020
Constitutional Affairs Member 2020

Last election

Miss Bakker won the 2020 Luzland general election in Steffisfelden with 29.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.8% more than the second placed candidate.
