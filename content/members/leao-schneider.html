--- title: Leao Schneider subtitle: Dr Leao Schneider is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Krneubruck constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Schneider has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an academic.
