--- title: Leao Schneider subtitle: Dr Leao Schneider is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Krneubruck constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Schneider has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an academic.

Parliamentary work

Dr Schneider has voted on 34 legislative proposals and spoke in 38 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2021
Constitutional Affairs Member 2021

Last election

Dr Schneider won the 2020 Luzland general election in Krneubruck with 28.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.9% more than the second placed candidate.
