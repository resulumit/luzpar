--- title: Leao Schneider subtitle: Dr Leao Schneider is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Krneubruck constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Schneider has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an academic.

Parliamentary work

Dr Schneider has voted on 52 legislative proposals and spoke in 30 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2012
Constitutional Affairs Member 2012

Last election

Dr Schneider won the 2020 Luzland general election in Krneubruck with 29.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.9% more than the second placed candidate.
