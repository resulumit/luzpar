--- title: Matteo Becker subtitle: Mr Matteo Becker is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Enkmelo constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mr Becker has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Becker has voted on 45 legislative proposals and spoke in 15 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2015
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2015

Last election

Mr Becker won the 2020 Luzland general election in Enkmelo with 28.5% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.899999999999999% more than the second placed candidate.
