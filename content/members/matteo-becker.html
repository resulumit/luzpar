--- title: Matteo Becker subtitle: Mr Matteo Becker is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Enkmelo constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mr Becker has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Becker has voted on 35 legislative proposals and spoke in 19 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2012
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2012

Last election

Mr Becker won the 2020 Luzland general election in Enkmelo with 28.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.4% more than the second placed candidate.
