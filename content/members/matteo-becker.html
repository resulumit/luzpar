--- title: Matteo Becker subtitle: Mr Matteo Becker is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Enkmelo constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mr Becker has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Becker has voted on 27 legislative proposals and spoke in 10 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2010
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2010

Last election

Mr Becker won the 2020 Luzland general election in Enkmelo with 34.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 8% more than the second placed candidate.
