--- title: Joyce Roux subtitle: Ms Joyce Roux is a member of parliament (Independent), representing the Dorventer constituency in North Luzland. ---

Ms Roux has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a trade union official.

Parliamentary work

Ms Roux has voted on 53 legislative proposals and spoke in 29 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.
