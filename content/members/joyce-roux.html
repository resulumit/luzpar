--- title: Joyce Roux subtitle: Ms Joyce Roux is a member of parliament (Independent), representing the Dorventer constituency in North Luzland. ---

Ms Roux has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a trade union official.

Parliamentary work

Ms Roux has voted on 33 legislative proposals and spoke in 15 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2013
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2013

Last election

Ms Roux won the 2020 Luzland general election in Dorventer with 30.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.9% more than the second placed candidate.
