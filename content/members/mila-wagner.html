--- title: Mila Wagner subtitle: Ms Mila Wagner is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Molenaarde constituency in North Luzland. ---

Ms Wagner has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Ms Wagner has voted on 57 legislative proposals and spoke in 16 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2017
Internal Affairs Member 2017

Last election

Ms Wagner won the 2020 Luzland general election in Molenaarde with 32.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 8% more than the second placed candidate.
