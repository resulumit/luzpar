--- title: Mila Wagner subtitle: Ms Mila Wagner is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Molenaarde constituency in North Luzland. ---

Ms Wagner has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Ms Wagner has voted on 30 legislative proposals and spoke in 43 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2017
Internal Affairs Member 2017

Last election

Ms Wagner won the 2020 Luzland general election in Molenaarde with 30.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.899999999999999% more than the second placed candidate.
