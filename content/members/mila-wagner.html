--- title: Mila Wagner subtitle: Ms Mila Wagner is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Molenaarde constituency in North Luzland. ---

Ms Wagner has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Ms Wagner has voted on 61 legislative proposals and spoke in 39 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2020
Internal Affairs Member 2020

Last election

Ms Wagner won the 2020 Luzland general election in Molenaarde with 31.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.9% more than the second placed candidate.
