--- title: Mila Wagner subtitle: Ms Mila Wagner is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Molenaarde constituency in North Luzland. ---

Ms Wagner has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Ms Wagner has voted on 54 legislative proposals and spoke in 7 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2019
Internal Affairs Member 2019

Last election

Ms Wagner won the 2020 Luzland general election in Molenaarde with 31.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5% more than the second placed candidate.
