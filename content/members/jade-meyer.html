--- title: Jade Meyer subtitle: Miss Jade Meyer is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Courveil constituency in South Luzland. ---

Miss Meyer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a librarian.

Parliamentary work

Miss Meyer has voted on 38 legislative proposals and spoke in 19 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2003
External Affairs Member 2003

Last election

Miss Meyer won the 2020 Luzland general election in Courveil with 31.5% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.9% more than the second placed candidate.
