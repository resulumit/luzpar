--- title: Luke Roth subtitle: Mr Luke Roth is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Hamwagen constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Roth has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Roth has voted on 64 legislative proposals and spoke in 7 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2007
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2007

Last election

Mr Roth won the 2020 Luzland general election in Hamwagen with 26.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.6% more than the second placed candidate.
