--- title: Simone Fischer subtitle: Mr Simone Fischer is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Einwil constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mr Fischer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Fischer has voted on 50 legislative proposals and spoke in 27 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2020
Constitutional Affairs Member 2020

Last election

Mr Fischer won the 2020 Luzland general election in Einwil with 32.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.2% more than the second placed candidate.
