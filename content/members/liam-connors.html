--- title: Liam Connors subtitle: Dr Liam Connors is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Sachsenräschen constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Connors has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a physician.

Parliamentary work

Dr Connors has voted on 26 legislative proposals and spoke in 20 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2017
Internal Affairs Member 2017

Last election

Dr Connors won the 2020 Luzland general election in Sachsenräschen with 31.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.8% more than the second placed candidate.
