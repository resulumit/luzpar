--- title: Gemma Martens subtitle: Dr Gemma Martens is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Denderren constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Martens has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a pharmacist.

Parliamentary work

Dr Martens has voted on 30 legislative proposals and spoke in 8 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.
