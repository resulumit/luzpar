--- title: Gemma Martens subtitle: Dr Gemma Martens is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Denderren constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Martens has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a pharmacist.

Parliamentary work

Dr Martens has voted on 38 legislative proposals and spoke in 43 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2021
External Affairs Member 2021

Last election

Dr Martens won the 2020 Luzland general election in Denderren with 32.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.8% more than the second placed candidate.
