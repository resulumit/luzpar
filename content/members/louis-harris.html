--- title: Louis Harris subtitle: Dr Louis Harris is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Colroux constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Harris has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a physician.
