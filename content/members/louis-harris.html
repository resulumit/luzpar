--- title: Louis Harris subtitle: Dr Louis Harris is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Colroux constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Harris has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a physician.

Parliamentary work

Dr Harris has voted on 36 legislative proposals and spoke in 38 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2018
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2018

Last election

Dr Harris won the 2020 Luzland general election in Colroux with 32.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.5% more than the second placed candidate.
