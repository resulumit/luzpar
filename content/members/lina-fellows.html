--- title: Lina Fellows subtitle: Ms Lina Fellows is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Walskirchen constituency in West Luzland. ---

Ms Fellows has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.
