--- title: Lina Fellows subtitle: Ms Lina Fellows is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Walskirchen constituency in West Luzland. ---

Ms Fellows has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Ms Fellows has voted on 33 legislative proposals and spoke in 15 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2007
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2007

Last election

Ms Fellows won the 2020 Luzland general election in Walskirchen with 32.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 7.1% more than the second placed candidate.
