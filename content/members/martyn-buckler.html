--- title: Martyn Buckler subtitle: Mr Martyn Buckler is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Roesringen constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Buckler has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Buckler has voted on 23 legislative proposals and spoke in 36 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures First Vice Chair 2021
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2021

Last election

Mr Buckler won the 2020 Luzland general election in Roesringen with 30.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.3% more than the second placed candidate.
