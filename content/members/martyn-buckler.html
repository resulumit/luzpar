--- title: Martyn Buckler subtitle: Mr Martyn Buckler is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Roesringen constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Buckler has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.
