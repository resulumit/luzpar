--- title: Martyn Buckler subtitle: Mr Martyn Buckler is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Roesringen constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Buckler has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Buckler has voted on 46 legislative proposals and spoke in 8 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures First Vice Chair 2020
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2020

Last election

Mr Buckler won the 2020 Luzland general election in Roesringen with 28.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.7% more than the second placed candidate.
