--- title: Piet Warren subtitle: Dr Piet Warren is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Eincona constituency in North Luzland. ---

Dr Warren has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a dentist.

Parliamentary work

Dr Warren has voted on 57 legislative proposals and spoke in 32 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2021
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2021

Last election

Dr Warren won the 2020 Luzland general election in Eincona with 31% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.7% more than the second placed candidate.
