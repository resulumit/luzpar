--- title: Piet Warren subtitle: Dr Piet Warren is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Eincona constituency in North Luzland. ---

Dr Warren has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a dentist.

Parliamentary work

Dr Warren has voted on 60 legislative proposals and spoke in 17 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2017
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2017

Last election

Dr Warren won the 2020 Luzland general election in Eincona with 29.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.599999999999998% more than the second placed candidate.
