--- title: Piet Warren subtitle: Dr Piet Warren is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Eincona constituency in North Luzland. ---

Dr Warren has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a dentist.

Parliamentary work

Dr Warren has voted on 52 legislative proposals and spoke in 43 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2007
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2007

Last election

Dr Warren won the 2020 Luzland general election in Eincona with 30.7% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.2% more than the second placed candidate.
