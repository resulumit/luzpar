--- title: Fred Lefebvre subtitle: Mr Fred Lefebvre is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Zuiderschoten constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Lefebvre has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Mr Lefebvre has voted on 26 legislative proposals and spoke in 17 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2015
Constitutional Affairs Member 2015

Last election

Mr Lefebvre won the 2020 Luzland general election in Zuiderschoten with 27.7% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.5% more than the second placed candidate.
