--- title: Fred Lefebvre subtitle: Mr Fred Lefebvre is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Zuiderschoten constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Lefebvre has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Mr Lefebvre has voted on 56 legislative proposals and spoke in 21 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2021
Constitutional Affairs Member 2021

Last election

Mr Lefebvre won the 2020 Luzland general election in Zuiderschoten with 27.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.599999999999998% more than the second placed candidate.
