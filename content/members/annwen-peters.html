--- title: Annwen Peters subtitle: Dr Annwen Peters is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Zelthofen constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Peters has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an academic.

Parliamentary work

Dr Peters has voted on 34 legislative proposals and spoke in 39 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2020
Internal Affairs Member 2020

Last election

Dr Peters won the 2020 Luzland general election in Zelthofen with 28.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.799999999999997% more than the second placed candidate.
