--- title: Annwen Peters subtitle: Dr Annwen Peters is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Zelthofen constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Peters has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an academic.

Parliamentary work

Dr Peters has voted on 40 legislative proposals and spoke in 34 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2016
Internal Affairs Member 2016

Last election

Dr Peters won the 2020 Luzland general election in Zelthofen with 30.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.3% more than the second placed candidate.
