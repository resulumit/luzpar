--- title: Annwen Peters subtitle: Dr Annwen Peters is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Zelthofen constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Peters has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an academic.

Parliamentary work

Dr Peters has voted on 29 legislative proposals and spoke in 42 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2021
Internal Affairs Member 2021

Last election

Dr Peters won the 2020 Luzland general election in Zelthofen with 33.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 8.3% more than the second placed candidate.
