--- title: Sally Schröder subtitle: Ms Sally Schröder is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Hooglare constituency in East Luzland. ---

Ms Schröder has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Ms Schröder has voted on 28 legislative proposals and spoke in 19 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Second Vice Chair 2021
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2021

Last election

Ms Schröder won the 2020 Luzland general election in Hooglare with 31.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.3% more than the second placed candidate.
