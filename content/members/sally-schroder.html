--- title: Sally Schröder subtitle: Ms Sally Schröder is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Hooglare constituency in East Luzland. ---

Ms Schröder has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Ms Schröder has voted on 25 legislative proposals and spoke in 11 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Second Vice Chair 2018
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2018

Last election

Ms Schröder won the 2020 Luzland general election in Hooglare with 29.7% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.2% more than the second placed candidate.
