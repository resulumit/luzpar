--- title: Christine Hendriks subtitle: Dr Christine Hendriks is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Gmunfurt constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Hendriks has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Dr Hendriks has voted on 52 legislative proposals and spoke in 22 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.
