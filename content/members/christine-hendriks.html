--- title: Christine Hendriks subtitle: Dr Christine Hendriks is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Gmunfurt constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Hendriks has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Dr Hendriks has voted on 44 legislative proposals and spoke in 12 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Member 2015
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2015

Last election

Dr Hendriks won the 2020 Luzland general election in Gmunfurt with 31.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 5.7% more than the second placed candidate.
