--- title: Léa Postma subtitle: Mrs Léa Postma is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Wijnrade constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mrs Postma has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Postma has voted on 50 legislative proposals and spoke in 46 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2021
Constitutional Affairs Member 2021

Last election

Mrs Postma won the 2020 Luzland general election in Wijnrade with 30% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.7% more than the second placed candidate.
