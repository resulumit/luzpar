--- title: Léa Postma subtitle: Mrs Léa Postma is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Wijnrade constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mrs Postma has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.
