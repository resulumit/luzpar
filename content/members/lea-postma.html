--- title: Léa Postma subtitle: Mrs Léa Postma is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Wijnrade constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mrs Postma has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Mrs Postma has voted on 54 legislative proposals and spoke in 46 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2011
Constitutional Affairs Member 2011

Last election

Mrs Postma won the 2020 Luzland general election in Wijnrade with 27.5% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.4% more than the second placed candidate.
