--- title: Patricia Bernard subtitle: Miss Patricia Bernard is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Gänsernten constituency in East Luzland. ---

Miss Bernard has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Miss Bernard has voted on 63 legislative proposals and spoke in 21 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2017
Standards and Procedures Member 2017

Last election

Miss Bernard won the 2020 Luzland general election in Gänsernten with 32.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.2% more than the second placed candidate.
