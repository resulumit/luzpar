--- title: Patricia Bernard subtitle: Miss Patricia Bernard is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Gänsernten constituency in East Luzland. ---

Miss Bernard has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Miss Bernard has voted on 56 legislative proposals and spoke in 20 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2020
Standards and Procedures Member 2020

Last election

Miss Bernard won the 2020 Luzland general election in Gänsernten with 27.4% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.799999999999997% more than the second placed candidate.
