--- title: Maël Dekker subtitle: Dr Maël Dekker is a member of parliament (Independent), representing the Granlarney constituency in North Luzland. ---

Dr Dekker has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a physician.

Parliamentary work

Dr Dekker has voted on 24 legislative proposals and spoke in 11 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2015
Internal Affairs Member 2015

Last election

Dr Dekker won the 2020 Luzland general election in Granlarney with 33.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 8% more than the second placed candidate.
