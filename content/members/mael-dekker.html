--- title: Maël Dekker subtitle: Dr Maël Dekker is a member of parliament (Independent), representing the Granlarney constituency in North Luzland. ---

Dr Dekker has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a physician.

Parliamentary work

Dr Dekker has voted on 31 legislative proposals and spoke in 44 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.
