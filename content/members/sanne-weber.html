--- title: Sanne Weber subtitle: Dr Sanne Weber is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Knokbergen constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Weber has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Dr Weber has voted on 31 legislative proposals and spoke in 36 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2015
External Affairs Member 2015

Last election

Dr Weber won the 2020 Luzland general election in Knokbergen with 30.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.7% more than the second placed candidate.
