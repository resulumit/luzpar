--- title: Sanne Weber subtitle: Dr Sanne Weber is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Knokbergen constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Weber has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.
