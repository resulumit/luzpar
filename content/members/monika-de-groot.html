--- title: Monika de Groot subtitle: Mrs Monika de Groot is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Dungarblayney constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mrs De Groot has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Mrs De Groot has voted on 40 legislative proposals and spoke in 29 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2016
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2016

Last election

Mrs De Groot won the 2020 Luzland general election in Dungarblayney with 28% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.800000000000001% more than the second placed candidate.
