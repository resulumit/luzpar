--- title: Monika de Groot subtitle: Mrs Monika de Groot is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Dungarblayney constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mrs De Groot has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Mrs De Groot has voted on 54 legislative proposals and spoke in 7 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2021
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2021

Last election

Mrs De Groot won the 2020 Luzland general election in Dungarblayney with 29.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3% more than the second placed candidate.
