--- title: Monika de Groot subtitle: Mrs Monika de Groot is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Dungarblayney constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mrs De Groot has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as an engineer.
