--- title: Johann Vos subtitle: Mr Johann Vos is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Carway constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mr Vos has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a trade union official.

Parliamentary work

Mr Vos has voted on 42 legislative proposals and spoke in 40 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.
