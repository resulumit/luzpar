--- title: Johann Vos subtitle: Mr Johann Vos is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Carway constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mr Vos has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a trade union official.

Parliamentary work

Mr Vos has voted on 33 legislative proposals and spoke in 8 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2020
Standards and Procedures Member 2020

Last election

Mr Vos won the 2020 Luzland general election in Carway with 29.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.1% more than the second placed candidate.
