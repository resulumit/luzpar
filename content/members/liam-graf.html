--- title: Liam Graf subtitle: Mr Liam Graf is a member of parliament (Independent), representing the Avirac constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Graf has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a journalist.

Parliamentary work

Mr Graf has voted on 59 legislative proposals and spoke in 27 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2003
Standards and Procedures Member 2003

Last election

Mr Graf won the 2020 Luzland general election in Avirac with 31.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.1% more than the second placed candidate.
