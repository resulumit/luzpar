--- title: Christian Schwarz subtitle: Mr Christian Schwarz is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Kerkenschaat constituency in West Luzland. ---

Mr Schwarz has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a pilot.

Parliamentary work

Mr Schwarz has voted on 53 legislative proposals and spoke in 27 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2017
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2017

Last election

Mr Schwarz won the 2020 Luzland general election in Kerkenschaat with 35% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 8.8% more than the second placed candidate.
