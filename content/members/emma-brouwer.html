--- title: Emma Brouwer subtitle: Ms Emma Brouwer is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Perpisier constituency in North Luzland. ---

Ms Brouwer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Ms Brouwer has voted on 32 legislative proposals and spoke in 45 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.
