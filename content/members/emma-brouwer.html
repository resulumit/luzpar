--- title: Emma Brouwer subtitle: Ms Emma Brouwer is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Perpisier constituency in North Luzland. ---

Ms Brouwer has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a judge.

Parliamentary work

Ms Brouwer has voted on 47 legislative proposals and spoke in 26 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2015
Standards and Procedures Member 2015

Last election

Ms Brouwer won the 2020 Luzland general election in Perpisier with 28.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2% more than the second placed candidate.
