--- title: Mia Janssen subtitle: Ms Mia Janssen is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Bischofring constituency in East Luzland. ---

Ms Janssen has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is her fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a journalist.

Parliamentary work

Ms Janssen has voted on 23 legislative proposals and spoke in 33 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.
