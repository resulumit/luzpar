--- title: Mia Janssen subtitle: Ms Mia Janssen is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Bischofring constituency in East Luzland. ---

Ms Janssen has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a journalist.

Parliamentary work

Ms Janssen has voted on 32 legislative proposals and spoke in 15 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Standards and Procedures Chair 2019
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2019

Last election

Ms Janssen won the 2020 Luzland general election in Bischofring with 28.7% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2% more than the second placed candidate.
