--- title: Ian van Dijk subtitle: Dr Ian van Dijk is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Steenmere constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Van Dijk has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an academic.

Parliamentary work

Dr Van Dijk has voted on 65 legislative proposals and spoke in 14 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2016
Internal Affairs Member 2016

Last election

Dr Van Dijk won the 2020 Luzland general election in Steenmere with 27.9% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 2.5% more than the second placed candidate.
