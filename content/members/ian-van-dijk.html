--- title: Ian van Dijk subtitle: Dr Ian van Dijk is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Steenmere constituency in South Luzland. ---

Dr Van Dijk has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an academic.

Parliamentary work

Dr Van Dijk has voted on 61 legislative proposals and spoke in 28 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2011
Internal Affairs Member 2011

Last election

Dr Van Dijk won the 2020 Luzland general election in Steenmere with 28.3% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.2% more than the second placed candidate.
