--- title: Jane Gerber subtitle: Dr Jane Gerber is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Forbergen constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Gerber has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is her first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.

Parliamentary work

Dr Gerber has voted on 49 legislative proposals and spoke in 35 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny First Vice Chair 2020
External Affairs Member 2020

Last election

Dr Gerber won the 2020 Luzland general election in Forbergen with 28.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4% more than the second placed candidate.
