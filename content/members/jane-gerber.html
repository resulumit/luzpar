--- title: Jane Gerber subtitle: Dr Jane Gerber is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Forbergen constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Gerber has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is her second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a lawyer.
