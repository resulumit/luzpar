--- title: Caroline Weber subtitle: Ms Caroline Weber is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Houizieu constituency in North Luzland. ---

Ms Weber has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is her third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Ms Weber has voted on 50 legislative proposals and spoke in 14 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.
