--- title: Caroline Weber subtitle: Ms Caroline Weber is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Houizieu constituency in North Luzland. ---

Ms Weber has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is her fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, she was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Ms Weber has voted on 59 legislative proposals and spoke in 46 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

She works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2001
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2001

Last election

Ms Weber won the 2020 Luzland general election in Houizieu with 30.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.7% more than the second placed candidate.
