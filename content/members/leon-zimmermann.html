--- title: Leon Zimmermann subtitle: Mr Leon Zimmermann is a member of parliament (Independent), representing the Vellhöring constituency in South Luzland. ---

Mr Zimmermann has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as an engineer.

Parliamentary work

Mr Zimmermann has voted on 48 legislative proposals and spoke in 39 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2020
Equalities and Human Rights Member 2020

Last election

Mr Zimmermann won the 2020 Luzland general election in Vellhöring with 31.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 6.4% more than the second placed candidate.
