--- title: Niels Richard subtitle: Dr Niels Richard is a member of parliament (Green), representing the Klingstal constituency in West Luzland. ---

Dr Richard has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a dentist.

Parliamentary work

Dr Richard has voted on 24 legislative proposals and spoke in 32 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Legislative Scrutiny Member 2014
External Affairs Member 2014

Last election

Dr Richard won the 2020 Luzland general election in Klingstal with 32.8% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 7.6% more than the second placed candidate.
