--- title: David Laurent subtitle: Mr David Laurent is a member of parliament (Labour), representing the Kreuzneuve constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Laurent has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2005, and this is his fourth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a businessperson.

Parliamentary work

Mr Laurent has voted on 65 legislative proposals and spoke in 10 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Constitutional Affairs Member 2011
Standards and Procedures Member 2011

Last election

Mr Laurent won the 2020 Luzland general election in Kreuzneuve with 28.1% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 1.7% more than the second placed candidate.
