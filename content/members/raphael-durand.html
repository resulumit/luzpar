--- title: Raphaël Durand subtitle: Mr Raphaël Durand is a member of parliament (Liberal), representing the Dornshafen constituency in East Luzland. ---

Mr Durand has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2020, and this is his first term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a journalist.

Parliamentary work

Mr Durand has voted on 59 legislative proposals and spoke in 23 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
Internal Affairs Member 2021
Constitutional Affairs Member 2021

Last election

Mr Durand won the 2020 Luzland general election in Dornshafen with 29.6% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 3.6% more than the second placed candidate.
