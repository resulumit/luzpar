--- title: Luca Schulz subtitle: Mr Luca Schulz is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Vollenzaal constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Schulz has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2015, and this is his second term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Mr Schulz has voted on 59 legislative proposals and spoke in 43 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2015
Standards and Procedures Member 2015

Last election

Mr Schulz won the 2020 Luzland general election in Vollenzaal with 34.2% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 7.8% more than the second placed candidate.
