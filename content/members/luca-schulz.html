--- title: Luca Schulz subtitle: Mr Luca Schulz is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Vollenzaal constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Schulz has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2000, and this is his fifth term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Mr Schulz has voted on 43 legislative proposals and spoke in 26 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2014
Standards and Procedures Member 2014

Last election

Mr Schulz won the 2020 Luzland general election in Vollenzaal with 28% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 0.600000000000001% more than the second placed candidate.
