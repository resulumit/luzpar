--- title: Luca Schulz subtitle: Mr Luca Schulz is a member of parliament (Conservative), representing the Vollenzaal constituency in North Luzland. ---

Mr Schulz has been a member of parliament since the general elections in 2010, and this is his third term in office. Before joining the Parliament of Luzland, he was working as a surgent.

Parliamentary work

Mr Schulz has voted on 50 legislative proposals and spoke in 29 plenary debates during the current parliamentary term.

He works in two parliamentary committees, as listed below.

Committee Position Since
External Affairs Member 2010
Standards and Procedures Member 2010

Last election

Mr Schulz won the 2020 Luzland general election in Vollenzaal with 30.7% of the valid votes cast in the constituency — 4.9% more than the second placed candidate.

Party Votes Share
Conservative 22,525 30.7%
