--- title: Bunlarney subtitle: Bunlarney is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Bunlarney is represented by Mrs Amanda Martin (Labour) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Martin was elected to represent Bunlarney, with 32.8% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
