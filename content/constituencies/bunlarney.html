--- title: Bunlarney subtitle: Bunlarney is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Bunlarney is represented by Mrs Amanda Martin (Labour) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Martin was elected to represent Bunlarney, with 33.4% of the valid votes. This was 5.4% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
