--- title: Bunlarney subtitle: Bunlarney is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Bunlarney is represented by Mrs Amanda Martin (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Martin was elected to represent Bunlarney, with 31.6% of the valid votes. This was 7.6% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
