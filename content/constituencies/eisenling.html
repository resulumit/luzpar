--- title: Eisenling subtitle: Eisenling is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Eisenling is represented by Dr Noah Ross (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Ross was elected to represent Eisenling, with 34% of the valid votes. This was 6.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
