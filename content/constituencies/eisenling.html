--- title: Eisenling subtitle: Eisenling is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Eisenling is represented by Dr Noah Ross (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Ross was elected to represent Eisenling, with 29.6% of the valid votes. This was 2.3% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
