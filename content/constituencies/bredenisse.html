--- title: Bredenisse subtitle: Bredenisse is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Bredenisse is represented by Dr Els Moreau (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Moreau was elected to represent Bredenisse, with 28.7% of the valid votes. This was 2.6% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
