--- title: Bredenisse subtitle: Bredenisse is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Bredenisse is represented by Dr Els Moreau (Labour) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Moreau was elected to represent Bredenisse, with 32% of the valid votes. This was 3.9% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
