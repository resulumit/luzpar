--- title: Riviervik subtitle: Riviervik is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Riviervik is represented by Ms Chantal de Boer (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms De Boer was elected to represent Riviervik, with 31.3% of the valid votes. This was 6.2% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
