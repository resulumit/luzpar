--- title: Riviervik subtitle: Riviervik is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Riviervik is represented by Ms Chantal de Boer (Labour) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms De Boer was elected to represent Riviervik, with 33.6% of the valid votes. This was 6% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
