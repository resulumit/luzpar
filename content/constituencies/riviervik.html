--- title: Riviervik subtitle: Riviervik is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Riviervik is represented by Ms Chantal de Boer (Labour) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms De Boer was elected to represent Riviervik, with 34% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
