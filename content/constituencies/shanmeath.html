--- title: Shanmeath subtitle: Shanmeath is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Shanmeath is represented by Mr Wouter Church (mps_party) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Church was elected to represent Shanmeath, with 28% of the valid votes. This was 1.4% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
