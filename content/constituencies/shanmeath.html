--- title: Shanmeath subtitle: Shanmeath is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Shanmeath is represented by Mr Wouter Church (Labour) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Church was elected to represent Shanmeath, with 30.3% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
