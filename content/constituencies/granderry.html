Member of parliament

Granderry is represented by Mrs Wilma Baumann (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Baumann was elected to represent Granderry, with 26.9% of the valid votes. This was 0.799999999999997% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
