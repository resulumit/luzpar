--- title: Granderry subtitle: Granderry is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Granderry is represented by Mrs Wilma Baumann (Green) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Baumann was elected to represent Granderry, with 31.7% of the valid votes. This was 3.8% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
