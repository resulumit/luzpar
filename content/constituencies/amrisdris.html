--- title: Amrisdris subtitle: Amrisdris is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Amrisdris is represented by Mr Franz Keller (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Keller was elected to represent Amrisdris, with 31% of the valid votes. This was 5.3% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
