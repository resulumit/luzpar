--- title: Amrisdris subtitle: Amrisdris is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Amrisdris is represented by Mr Franz Keller (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Keller was elected to represent Amrisdris, with 29.7% of the valid votes. This was 0.800000000000001% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
