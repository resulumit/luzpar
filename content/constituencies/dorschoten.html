--- title: Dorschoten subtitle: Dorschoten is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Dorschoten is represented by Mr Arjan van Wijk (mps_party) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Van Wijk was elected to represent Dorschoten, with 34.5% of the valid votes. This was 6.9% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
