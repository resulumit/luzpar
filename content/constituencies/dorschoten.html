--- title: Dorschoten subtitle: Dorschoten is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Dorschoten is represented by Mr Arjan van Wijk (Conservative) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Van Wijk was elected to represent Dorschoten, with 31% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
Conservative 33,823 31%
