--- title: Dorschoten subtitle: Dorschoten is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Dorschoten is represented by Mr Arjan van Wijk (Conservative) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Van Wijk was elected to represent Dorschoten, with 29.8% of the valid votes. This was 1.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
