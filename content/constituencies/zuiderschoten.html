--- title: Zuiderschoten subtitle: Zuiderschoten is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Zuiderschoten is represented by Mr Fred Lefebvre (Liberal) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Lefebvre was elected to represent Zuiderschoten, with 32.7% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
