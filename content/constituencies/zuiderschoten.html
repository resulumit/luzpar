--- title: Zuiderschoten subtitle: Zuiderschoten is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Zuiderschoten is represented by Mr Fred Lefebvre (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Lefebvre was elected to represent Zuiderschoten, with 27.7% of the valid votes. This was 2.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
