--- title: Zuiderschoten subtitle: Zuiderschoten is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Zuiderschoten is represented by Mr Fred Lefebvre (Liberal) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Lefebvre was elected to represent Zuiderschoten, with 32.5% of the valid votes. This was 7.2% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
