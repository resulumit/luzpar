--- title: Rivierren subtitle: Rivierren is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Rivierren is represented by Mr Hugo Moser (mps_party) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Moser was elected to represent Rivierren, with 29.2% of the valid votes. This was 0.800000000000001% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
