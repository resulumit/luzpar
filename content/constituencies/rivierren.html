--- title: Rivierren subtitle: Rivierren is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Rivierren is represented by Mr Hugo Moser (Labour) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Moser was elected to represent Rivierren, with 28.3% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
