--- title: Rivierren subtitle: Rivierren is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Rivierren is represented by Mr Hugo Moser (Labour) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Moser was elected to represent Rivierren, with 28.8% of the valid votes. This was 1.4% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
