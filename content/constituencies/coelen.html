--- title: Coelen subtitle: Coelen is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Coelen is represented by Ms Louise Müller (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Müller was elected to represent Coelen, with 31% of the valid votes. This was 4.6% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
