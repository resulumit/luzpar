--- title: Coelen subtitle: Coelen is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Coelen is represented by Ms Louise Müller (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Müller was elected to represent Coelen, with 27.6% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
