--- title: Dillon subtitle: Dillon is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Dillon is represented by Dr Rose Barnes (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Barnes was elected to represent Dillon, with 29.4% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
