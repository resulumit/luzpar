--- title: Dillon subtitle: Dillon is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Dillon is represented by Dr Rose Barnes (Liberal) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Barnes was elected to represent Dillon, with 30.1% of the valid votes. This was 3% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
