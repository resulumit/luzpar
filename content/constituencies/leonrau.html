--- title: Leonrau subtitle: Leonrau is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Leonrau is represented by Miss Lina Booth (Liberal) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Miss Booth was elected to represent Leonrau, with 33.5% of the valid votes. This was 5.7% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
