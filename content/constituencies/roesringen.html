--- title: Roesringen subtitle: Roesringen is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Roesringen is represented by Mr Martyn Buckler (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Buckler was elected to represent Roesringen, with 29.6% of the valid votes. This was 3.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
