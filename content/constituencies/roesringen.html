--- title: Roesringen subtitle: Roesringen is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Roesringen is represented by Mr Martyn Buckler (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Buckler was elected to represent Roesringen, with 30.2% of the valid votes. This was 6.3% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
