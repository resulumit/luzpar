Member of parliament

Avirac is represented by Mr Liam Graf (mps_party) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Graf was elected to represent Avirac, with 27.3% of the valid votes. This was 0.800000000000001% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
