--- title: Avirac subtitle: Avirac is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Avirac is represented by Mr Liam Graf (Independent) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Graf was elected to represent Avirac, with 27.9% of the valid votes.
