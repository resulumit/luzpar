--- title: Avirac subtitle: Avirac is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Avirac is represented by Mr Liam Graf (Independent) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Graf was elected to represent Avirac, with 33.8% of the valid votes. This was 6.8% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
