--- title: Roesberg subtitle: Roesberg is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Roesberg is represented by Mr David Hoffmann (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Hoffmann was elected to represent Roesberg, with 27.8% of the valid votes. This was 2% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
