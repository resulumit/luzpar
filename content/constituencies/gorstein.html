--- title: Gorstein subtitle: Gorstein is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Gorstein is represented by Mr Wolfgang Visser (Labour) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Visser was elected to represent Gorstein, with 30.8% of the valid votes. This was 6.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
