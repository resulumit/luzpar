--- title: Gorstein subtitle: Gorstein is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Gorstein is represented by Mr Wolfgang Visser (mps_party) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Visser was elected to represent Gorstein, with 27.6% of the valid votes. This was 1.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
