--- title: Balnon subtitle: Balnon is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Balnon is represented by Dr Elfriede Meyer (mps_party) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Meyer was elected to represent Balnon, with 30.6% of the valid votes. This was 1.2% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
