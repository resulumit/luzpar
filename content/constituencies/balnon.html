--- title: Balnon subtitle: Balnon is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Balnon is represented by Dr Elfriede Meyer (Liberal) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Meyer was elected to represent Balnon, with 29.4% of the valid votes. This was 4.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
