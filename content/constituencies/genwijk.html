--- title: Genwijk subtitle: Genwijk is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Genwijk is represented by Ms Alice Garcia (Liberal) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Garcia was elected to represent Genwijk, with 34.3% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
