--- title: Genwijk subtitle: Genwijk is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Genwijk is represented by Ms Alice Garcia (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Garcia was elected to represent Genwijk, with 27.9% of the valid votes. This was 3.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
