--- title: Hamwagen subtitle: Hamwagen is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Hamwagen is represented by Mr Luke Roth (Liberal) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Roth was elected to represent Hamwagen, with 30.6% of the valid votes. This was 4.6% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
