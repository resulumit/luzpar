--- title: Hamwagen subtitle: Hamwagen is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Hamwagen is represented by Mr Luke Roth (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Roth was elected to represent Hamwagen, with 30.7% of the valid votes. This was 3.7% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
