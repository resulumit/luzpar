--- title: Hamwagen subtitle: Hamwagen is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Hamwagen is represented by Mr Luke Roth (Liberal) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Roth was elected to represent Hamwagen, with 30.6% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
