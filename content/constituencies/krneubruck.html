--- title: Krneubruck subtitle: Krneubruck is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Krneubruck is represented by Dr Leao Schneider (Green) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Schneider was elected to represent Krneubruck, with 30.4% of the valid votes. This was 5.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
