--- title: Krneubruck subtitle: Krneubruck is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Krneubruck is represented by Dr Leao Schneider (Green) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Schneider was elected to represent Krneubruck, with 31.2% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
