--- title: Ligny subtitle: Ligny is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Ligny is represented by Dr Jules Brunner (Conservative) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Brunner was elected to represent Ligny, with 32.5% of the valid votes. This was 4.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
