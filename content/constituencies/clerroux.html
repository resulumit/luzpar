--- title: Clerroux subtitle: Clerroux is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Clerroux is represented by Mr Helmut Mercier (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Mercier was elected to represent Clerroux, with 33.2% of the valid votes.
