--- title: Colroux subtitle: Colroux is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Colroux is represented by Dr Louis Harris (mps_party) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Harris was elected to represent Colroux, with 32.4% of the valid votes. This was 4.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
