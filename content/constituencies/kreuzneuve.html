--- title: Kreuzneuve subtitle: Kreuzneuve is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Kreuzneuve is represented by Mr David Laurent (mps_party) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Laurent was elected to represent Kreuzneuve, with 34.5% of the valid votes. This was 7.6% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
