--- title: Kreuzneuve subtitle: Kreuzneuve is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Kreuzneuve is represented by Mr David Laurent (Labour) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Laurent was elected to represent Kreuzneuve, with 31.7% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
Labour 21,110 31.7%
Green 18,576 27.9%
