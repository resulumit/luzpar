--- title: Culemdam subtitle: Culemdam is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Culemdam is represented by Mrs Evelyn Vink (mps_party) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Vink was elected to represent Culemdam, with 32.1% of the valid votes. This was 5.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
