--- title: Culemdam subtitle: Culemdam is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Culemdam is represented by Mrs Evelyn Vink (Labour) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Vink was elected to represent Culemdam, with 30.9% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
Labour 28,777 30.9%
