--- title: Culemdam subtitle: Culemdam is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Culemdam is represented by Mrs Evelyn Vink (Labour) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Vink was elected to represent Culemdam, with 29.8% of the valid votes. This was 2.7% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
