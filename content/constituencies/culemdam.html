--- title: Culemdam subtitle: Culemdam is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Culemdam is represented by Mrs Evelyn Vink (Labour) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Vink was elected to represent Culemdam, with 29.5% of the valid votes. This was 1.2% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
