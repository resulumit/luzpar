--- title: Hooglare subtitle: Hooglare is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Hooglare is represented by Ms Sally Schröder (Conservative) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Schröder was elected to represent Hooglare, with 31.4% of the valid votes. This was 6.3% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
