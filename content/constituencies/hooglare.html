--- title: Hooglare subtitle: Hooglare is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Hooglare is represented by Ms Sally Schröder (Conservative) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Schröder was elected to represent Hooglare, with 32.9% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
