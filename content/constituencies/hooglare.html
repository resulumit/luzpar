--- title: Hooglare subtitle: Hooglare is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Hooglare is represented by Ms Sally Schröder (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Schröder was elected to represent Hooglare, with 27.2% of the valid votes. This was 1.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
