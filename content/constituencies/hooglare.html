--- title: Hooglare subtitle: Hooglare is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Hooglare is represented by Ms Sally Schröder (mps_party) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Schröder was elected to represent Hooglare, with 32% of the valid votes. This was 6.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
