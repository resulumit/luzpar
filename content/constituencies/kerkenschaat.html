--- title: Kerkenschaat subtitle: Kerkenschaat is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Kerkenschaat is represented by Mr Christian Schwarz (Green) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Schwarz was elected to represent Kerkenschaat, with 33.1% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
Green 30,383 33.1%
