--- title: Kerkenschaat subtitle: Kerkenschaat is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Kerkenschaat is represented by Mr Christian Schwarz (mps_party) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Schwarz was elected to represent Kerkenschaat, with 27.4% of the valid votes. This was 1.9% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
