--- title: Alenyonne subtitle: Alenyonne is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Alenyonne is represented by Mrs Carla Richter (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Richter was elected to represent Alenyonne, with 30.5% of the valid votes. This was 1.8% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
