--- title: Alenyonne subtitle: Alenyonne is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Alenyonne is represented by Mrs Carla Richter (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Richter was elected to represent Alenyonne, with 27.9% of the valid votes. This was 2% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
