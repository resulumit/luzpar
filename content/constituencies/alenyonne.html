--- title: Alenyonne subtitle: Alenyonne is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Alenyonne is represented by Mrs Carla Richter (Liberal) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Richter was elected to represent Alenyonne, with 28.4% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
