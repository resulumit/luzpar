--- title: Vollenzaal subtitle: Vollenzaal is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Vollenzaal is represented by Mr Luca Schulz (Conservative) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Schulz was elected to represent Vollenzaal, with 28% of the valid votes. This was 0.600000000000001% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
