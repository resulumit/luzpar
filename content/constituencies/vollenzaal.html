--- title: Vollenzaal subtitle: Vollenzaal is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Vollenzaal is represented by Mr Luca Schulz (mps_party) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Schulz was elected to represent Vollenzaal, with 30.7% of the valid votes. This was 4.9% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
