--- title: Vollenzaal subtitle: Vollenzaal is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Vollenzaal is represented by Mr Luca Schulz (Conservative) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Schulz was elected to represent Vollenzaal, with 28.3% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
