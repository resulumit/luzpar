--- title: Besanmans subtitle: Besanmans is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Besanmans is represented by Mr Walter Schneider (Labour) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Schneider was elected to represent Besanmans, with 33.3% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
Labour 20,574 33.3%
Liberal 16,047 26%
