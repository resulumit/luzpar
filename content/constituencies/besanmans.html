--- title: Besanmans subtitle: Besanmans is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Besanmans is represented by Mr Walter Schneider (Labour) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Schneider was elected to represent Besanmans, with 29.6% of the valid votes. This was 2.7% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
