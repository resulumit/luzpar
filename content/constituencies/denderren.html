--- title: Denderren subtitle: Denderren is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Denderren is represented by Dr Gemma Martens (Conservative) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Martens was elected to represent Denderren, with 28.3% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
