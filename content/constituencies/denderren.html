--- title: Denderren subtitle: Denderren is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Denderren is represented by Dr Gemma Martens (Conservative) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Martens was elected to represent Denderren, with 32.1% of the valid votes. This was 4.2% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
