--- title: Zelthofen subtitle: Zelthofen is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Zelthofen is represented by Dr Annwen Peters (mps_party) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Peters was elected to represent Zelthofen, with 32% of the valid votes. This was 3.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
