--- title: Zelthofen subtitle: Zelthofen is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Zelthofen is represented by Dr Annwen Peters (Liberal) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Peters was elected to represent Zelthofen, with 30.9% of the valid votes. This was 4.3% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
