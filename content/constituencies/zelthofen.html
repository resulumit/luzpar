Member of parliament

Zelthofen is represented by Dr Annwen Peters (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Peters was elected to represent Zelthofen, with 28.9% of the valid votes. This was 0.799999999999997% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
