--- title: Zelthofen subtitle: Zelthofen is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Zelthofen is represented by Dr Annwen Peters (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Peters was elected to represent Zelthofen, with 29.2% of the valid votes. This was 1.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
