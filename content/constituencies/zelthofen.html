--- title: Zelthofen subtitle: Zelthofen is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Zelthofen is represented by Dr Annwen Peters (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Peters was elected to represent Zelthofen, with 33.2% of the valid votes. This was 8.3% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
