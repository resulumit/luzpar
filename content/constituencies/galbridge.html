--- title: Galbridge subtitle: Galbridge is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Galbridge is represented by Ms Glenn Widmer (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Widmer was elected to represent Galbridge, with 33.7% of the valid votes. This was 8.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
