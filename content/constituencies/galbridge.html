--- title: Galbridge subtitle: Galbridge is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Galbridge is represented by Ms Glenn Widmer (Green) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Widmer was elected to represent Galbridge, with 31.5% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
Green 23,884 31.5%
Labour 20,540 27.1%
Conservative 17,869 23.6%
