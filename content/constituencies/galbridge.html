--- title: Galbridge subtitle: Galbridge is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Galbridge is represented by Ms Glenn Widmer (Green) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Widmer was elected to represent Galbridge, with 34.6% of the valid votes. This was 8% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
