--- title: Niedernholz subtitle: Niedernholz is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Niedernholz is represented by Mr Andreas Tucker (Green) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Tucker was elected to represent Niedernholz, with 28.9% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
