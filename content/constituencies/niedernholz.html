--- title: Niedernholz subtitle: Niedernholz is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Niedernholz is represented by Mr Andreas Tucker (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Tucker was elected to represent Niedernholz, with 29.1% of the valid votes. This was 3.2% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
