--- title: Niedernholz subtitle: Niedernholz is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Niedernholz is represented by Mr Andreas Tucker (Green) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Tucker was elected to represent Niedernholz, with 32.1% of the valid votes. This was 5.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
