--- title: Dorventer subtitle: Dorventer is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Dorventer is represented by Ms Joyce Roux (Independent) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Roux was elected to represent Dorventer, with 33.4% of the valid votes. This was 5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
