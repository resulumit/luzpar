--- title: Rheidrisio subtitle: Rheidrisio is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Rheidrisio is represented by Miss Elisabeth Huber (Labour) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Miss Huber was elected to represent Rheidrisio, with 33.9% of the valid votes. This was 7.4% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
