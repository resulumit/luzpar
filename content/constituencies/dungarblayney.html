--- title: Dungarblayney subtitle: Dungarblayney is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Dungarblayney is represented by Mrs Monika de Groot (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs De Groot was elected to represent Dungarblayney, with 28% of the valid votes. This was 0.800000000000001% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
