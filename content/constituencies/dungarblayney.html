--- title: Dungarblayney subtitle: Dungarblayney is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Dungarblayney is represented by Mrs Monika de Groot (Liberal) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs De Groot was elected to represent Dungarblayney, with 31.2% of the valid votes. This was 7.2% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
