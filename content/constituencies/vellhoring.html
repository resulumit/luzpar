--- title: Vellhöring subtitle: Vellhöring is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Vellhöring is represented by Mr Leon Zimmermann (mps_party) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Zimmermann was elected to represent Vellhöring, with 32.3% of the valid votes. This was 4.2% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
