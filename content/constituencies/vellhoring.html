--- title: Vellhöring subtitle: Vellhöring is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Vellhöring is represented by Mr Leon Zimmermann (Independent) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Zimmermann was elected to represent Vellhöring, with 28.2% of the valid votes.
