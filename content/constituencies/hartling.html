--- title: Hartling subtitle: Hartling is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Hartling is represented by Mr Joke Meijer (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Meijer was elected to represent Hartling, with 28.3% of the valid votes. This was 1.7% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
