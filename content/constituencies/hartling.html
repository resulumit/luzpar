--- title: Hartling subtitle: Hartling is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Hartling is represented by Mr Joke Meijer (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Meijer was elected to represent Hartling, with 31.4% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
