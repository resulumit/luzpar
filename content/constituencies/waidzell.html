--- title: Waidzell subtitle: Waidzell is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Waidzell is represented by Mr Herbert Wolf (Independent) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Wolf was elected to represent Waidzell, with 32.5% of the valid votes.
