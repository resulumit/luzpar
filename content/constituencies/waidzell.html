Member of parliament

Waidzell is represented by Mr Herbert Wolf (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Wolf was elected to represent Waidzell, with 26.2% of the valid votes. This was 0.599999999999998% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
