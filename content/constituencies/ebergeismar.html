--- title: Ebergeismar subtitle: Ebergeismar is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Ebergeismar is represented by Dr Amita Jansen (mps_party) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Jansen was elected to represent Ebergeismar, with 30.4% of the valid votes. This was 2.8% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
