--- title: Ebergeismar subtitle: Ebergeismar is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Ebergeismar is represented by Dr Amita Jansen (Conservative) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Jansen was elected to represent Ebergeismar, with 30.9% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
