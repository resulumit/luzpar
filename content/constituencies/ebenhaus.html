--- title: Ebenhaus subtitle: Ebenhaus is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Ebenhaus is represented by Mr Karl Connor (Liberal) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Connor was elected to represent Ebenhaus, with 34.4% of the valid votes. This was 8.3% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
