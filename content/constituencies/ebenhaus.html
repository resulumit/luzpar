--- title: Ebenhaus subtitle: Ebenhaus is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Ebenhaus is represented by Mr Karl Connor (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Connor was elected to represent Ebenhaus, with 30.3% of the valid votes. This was 1.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
