--- title: Ebenhaus subtitle: Ebenhaus is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Ebenhaus is represented by Mr Karl Connor (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Connor was elected to represent Ebenhaus, with 31.8% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
