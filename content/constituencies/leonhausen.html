--- title: Leonhausen subtitle: Leonhausen is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Leonhausen is represented by Ms Mia Wyss (Labour) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Wyss was elected to represent Leonhausen, with 26.5% of the valid votes. This was 0.8% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
