--- title: Walskirchen subtitle: Walskirchen is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Walskirchen is represented by Ms Lina Fellows (Labour) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Fellows was elected to represent Walskirchen, with 29.3% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
