--- title: Walskirchen subtitle: Walskirchen is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Walskirchen is represented by Ms Lina Fellows (Labour) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Fellows was elected to represent Walskirchen, with 32.3% of the valid votes. This was 7.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
