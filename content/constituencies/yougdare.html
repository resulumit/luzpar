--- title: Yougdare subtitle: Yougdare is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Yougdare is represented by Mr Tim de Vries (Green) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr De Vries was elected to represent Yougdare, with 30.8% of the valid votes. This was 5.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
