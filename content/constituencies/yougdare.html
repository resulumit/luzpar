--- title: Yougdare subtitle: Yougdare is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Yougdare is represented by Mr Tim de Vries (Green) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr De Vries was elected to represent Yougdare, with 32.2% of the valid votes.
