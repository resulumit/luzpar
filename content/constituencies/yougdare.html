--- title: Yougdare subtitle: Yougdare is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Yougdare is represented by Mr Tim de Vries (mps_party) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr De Vries was elected to represent Yougdare, with 30.8% of the valid votes. This was 2.4% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
