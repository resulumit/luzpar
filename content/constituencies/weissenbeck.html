--- title: Weißenbeck subtitle: Weißenbeck is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Weißenbeck is represented by Miss Annemarie Michel (Green) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Miss Michel was elected to represent Weißenbeck, with 31.2% of the valid votes.
