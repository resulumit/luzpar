--- title: Weißenbeck subtitle: Weißenbeck is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Weißenbeck is represented by Miss Annemarie Michel (Green) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Miss Michel was elected to represent Weißenbeck, with 28.4% of the valid votes. This was 2.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
