--- title: Weißenbeck subtitle: Weißenbeck is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Weißenbeck is represented by Miss Annemarie Michel (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Miss Michel was elected to represent Weißenbeck, with 34.3% of the valid votes. This was 7.9% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
