--- title: Wolfsfurt subtitle: Wolfsfurt is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Wolfsfurt is represented by Dr Bianca Leroy (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Leroy was elected to represent Wolfsfurt, with 27.8% of the valid votes. This was 0.800000000000001% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
