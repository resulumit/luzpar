--- title: Perpisier subtitle: Perpisier is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Perpisier is represented by Ms Emma Brouwer (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Brouwer was elected to represent Perpisier, with 29.8% of the valid votes. This was 2.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
