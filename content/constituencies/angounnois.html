--- title: Angounnois subtitle: Angounnois is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Angounnois is represented by Dr Salihu Lambert (Green) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Lambert was elected to represent Angounnois, with 32.1% of the valid votes. This was 6.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
