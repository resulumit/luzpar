--- title: Angounnois subtitle: Angounnois is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Angounnois is represented by Dr Salihu Lambert (Green) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Lambert was elected to represent Angounnois, with 31% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
