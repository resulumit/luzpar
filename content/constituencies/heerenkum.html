--- title: Heerenkum subtitle: Heerenkum is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Heerenkum is represented by Mr Thomas Schäfer (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Schäfer was elected to represent Heerenkum, with 31% of the valid votes. This was 3.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
