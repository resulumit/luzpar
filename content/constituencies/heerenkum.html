--- title: Heerenkum subtitle: Heerenkum is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Heerenkum is represented by Mr Thomas Schäfer (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Schäfer was elected to represent Heerenkum, with 29.7% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
