--- title: Kilnard subtitle: Kilnard is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Kilnard is represented by Dr Emilia Bauer (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Bauer was elected to represent Kilnard, with 31.3% of the valid votes. This was 2.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
