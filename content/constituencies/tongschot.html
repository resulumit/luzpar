--- title: Tongschot subtitle: Tongschot is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Tongschot is represented by Miss Sofia Girard (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Miss Girard was elected to represent Tongschot, with 28.4% of the valid votes. This was 1.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
