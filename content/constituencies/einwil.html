--- title: Einwil subtitle: Einwil is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Einwil is represented by Mr Simone Fischer (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Fischer was elected to represent Einwil, with 30.6% of the valid votes. This was 2.2% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
