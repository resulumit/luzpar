--- title: Einwil subtitle: Einwil is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Einwil is represented by Mr Simone Fischer (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Fischer was elected to represent Einwil, with 32.1% of the valid votes. This was 4.2% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
