Member of parliament

Haarlem is represented by Ms Helga Patel (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Patel was elected to represent Haarlem, with 28% of the valid votes. This was 0.899999999999999% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
