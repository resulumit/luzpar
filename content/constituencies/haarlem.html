--- title: Haarlem subtitle: Haarlem is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Haarlem is represented by Ms Helga Patel (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Patel was elected to represent Haarlem, with 31.9% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
