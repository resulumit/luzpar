--- title: Haarlem subtitle: Haarlem is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Haarlem is represented by Ms Helga Patel (Liberal) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Patel was elected to represent Haarlem, with 31.6% of the valid votes. This was 6.3% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
