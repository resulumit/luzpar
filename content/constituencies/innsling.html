--- title: Innsling subtitle: Innsling is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Innsling is represented by Mrs Elizabeth Zimmermann (Labour) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Zimmermann was elected to represent Innsling, with 32.8% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
