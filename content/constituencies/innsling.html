--- title: Innsling subtitle: Innsling is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Innsling is represented by Mrs Elizabeth Zimmermann (Labour) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Zimmermann was elected to represent Innsling, with 31% of the valid votes. This was 3.7% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
