--- title: Innsling subtitle: Innsling is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Innsling is represented by Mrs Elizabeth Zimmermann (mps_party) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Zimmermann was elected to represent Innsling, with 31.5% of the valid votes. This was 3.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
