--- title: Eincona subtitle: Eincona is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Eincona is represented by Dr Piet Warren (Labour) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Warren was elected to represent Eincona, with 30.7% of the valid votes. This was 6.2% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
