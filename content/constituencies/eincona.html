--- title: Eincona subtitle: Eincona is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Eincona is represented by Dr Piet Warren (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Warren was elected to represent Eincona, with 31% of the valid votes. This was 3.7% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
