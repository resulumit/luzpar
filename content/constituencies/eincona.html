Member of parliament

Eincona is represented by Dr Piet Warren (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Warren was elected to represent Eincona, with 29.2% of the valid votes. This was 0.599999999999998% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
