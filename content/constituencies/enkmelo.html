--- title: Enkmelo subtitle: Enkmelo is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Enkmelo is represented by Mr Matteo Becker (mps_party) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Becker was elected to represent Enkmelo, with 34.8% of the valid votes. This was 8% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
