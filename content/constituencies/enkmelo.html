--- title: Enkmelo subtitle: Enkmelo is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Enkmelo is represented by Mr Matteo Becker (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Becker was elected to represent Enkmelo, with 28.5% of the valid votes. This was 0.899999999999999% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
