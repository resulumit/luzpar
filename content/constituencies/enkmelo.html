--- title: Enkmelo subtitle: Enkmelo is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Enkmelo is represented by Mr Matteo Becker (Labour) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Becker was elected to represent Enkmelo, with 31.6% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
Labour 26,416 31.6%
Conservative 20,604 24.7%
