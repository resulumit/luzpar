--- title: Enkmelo subtitle: Enkmelo is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Enkmelo is represented by Mr Matteo Becker (Labour) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Becker was elected to represent Enkmelo, with 32.2% of the valid votes. This was 6.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
