--- title: Borgny subtitle: Borgny is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Borgny is represented by Mr Jamie Fournier (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Fournier was elected to represent Borgny, with 29.9% of the valid votes. This was 3.9% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
