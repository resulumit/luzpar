--- title: Borgny subtitle: Borgny is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Borgny is represented by Mr Jamie Fournier (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Fournier was elected to represent Borgny, with 29.2% of the valid votes. This was 1.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
