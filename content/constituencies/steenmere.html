--- title: Steenmere subtitle: Steenmere is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Steenmere is represented by Dr Ian van Dijk (Liberal) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Van Dijk was elected to represent Steenmere, with 30.3% of the valid votes. This was 6% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
