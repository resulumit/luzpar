--- title: Steenmere subtitle: Steenmere is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Steenmere is represented by Dr Ian van Dijk (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Van Dijk was elected to represent Steenmere, with 34.3% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
