--- title: Steenmere subtitle: Steenmere is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Steenmere is represented by Dr Ian van Dijk (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Van Dijk was elected to represent Steenmere, with 27.9% of the valid votes. This was 2.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
