--- title: Listry subtitle: Listry is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Listry is represented by Miss Anna van der Berg (mps_party) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Miss Van der Berg was elected to represent Listry, with 31.7% of the valid votes. This was 6.7% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
