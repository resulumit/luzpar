--- title: Listry subtitle: Listry is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Listry is represented by Miss Anna van der Berg (Conservative) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Miss Van der Berg was elected to represent Listry, with 29% of the valid votes. This was 2.3% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
