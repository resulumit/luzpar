--- title: Zotburg subtitle: Zotburg is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Zotburg is represented by Mrs Sophie Bos (Independent) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Bos was elected to represent Zotburg, with 31.3% of the valid votes. This was 5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
