--- title: Zotburg subtitle: Zotburg is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Zotburg is represented by Mrs Sophie Bos (Independent) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Bos was elected to represent Zotburg, with 30.5% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
Independent 25,405 30.5%
