--- title: Courveil subtitle: Courveil is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Courveil is represented by Miss Jade Meyer (Liberal) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Miss Meyer was elected to represent Courveil, with 30.4% of the valid votes. This was 2.4% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
