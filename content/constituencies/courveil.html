--- title: Courveil subtitle: Courveil is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Courveil is represented by Miss Jade Meyer (mps_party) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Miss Meyer was elected to represent Courveil, with 27.7% of the valid votes. This was 1.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
