--- title: Bischofring subtitle: Bischofring is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Bischofring is represented by Ms Mia Janssen (Liberal) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Janssen was elected to represent Bischofring, with 31.8% of the valid votes. This was 5.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
