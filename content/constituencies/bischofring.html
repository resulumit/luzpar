--- title: Bischofring subtitle: Bischofring is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Bischofring is represented by Ms Mia Janssen (Liberal) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Janssen was elected to represent Bischofring, with 30.3% of the valid votes.
