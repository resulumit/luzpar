--- title: Forbergen subtitle: Forbergen is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Forbergen is represented by Dr Jane Gerber (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Gerber was elected to represent Forbergen, with 26.9% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
