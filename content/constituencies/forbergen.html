--- title: Forbergen subtitle: Forbergen is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Forbergen is represented by Dr Jane Gerber (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Gerber was elected to represent Forbergen, with 33.2% of the valid votes. This was 6.6% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
