--- title: Arford subtitle: Arford is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Arford is represented by Dr Philip Collins (mps_party) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Collins was elected to represent Arford, with 29.4% of the valid votes. This was 2.6% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
