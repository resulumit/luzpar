--- title: Arford subtitle: Arford is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Arford is represented by Dr Philip Collins (mps_party) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Collins was elected to represent Arford, with 30.5% of the valid votes. This was 2.4% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
