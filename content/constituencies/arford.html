--- title: Arford subtitle: Arford is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Arford is represented by Dr Philip Collins (Conservative) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Collins was elected to represent Arford, with 33.5% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
Conservative 35,088 33.5%
Green 28,421 27.1%
