--- title: Heusbeke subtitle: Heusbeke is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Heusbeke is represented by Dr Suzanne Robert (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Robert was elected to represent Heusbeke, with 30.1% of the valid votes.
