--- title: Zoteinde subtitle: Zoteinde is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Zoteinde is represented by Dr Miranda Wood (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Wood was elected to represent Zoteinde, with 30.3% of the valid votes. This was 2.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
