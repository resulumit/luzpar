--- title: Zoteinde subtitle: Zoteinde is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Zoteinde is represented by Dr Miranda Wood (Liberal) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Wood was elected to represent Zoteinde, with 32.2% of the valid votes. This was 4.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
