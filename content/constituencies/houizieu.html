--- title: Houizieu subtitle: Houizieu is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Houizieu is represented by Ms Caroline Weber (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Weber was elected to represent Houizieu, with 32% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
