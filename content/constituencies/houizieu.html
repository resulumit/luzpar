--- title: Houizieu subtitle: Houizieu is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Houizieu is represented by Ms Caroline Weber (mps_party) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Weber was elected to represent Houizieu, with 30.1% of the valid votes. This was 3.9% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
