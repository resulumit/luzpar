--- title: Houizieu subtitle: Houizieu is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Houizieu is represented by Ms Caroline Weber (Liberal) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Weber was elected to represent Houizieu, with 28.5% of the valid votes. This was 1.4% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
