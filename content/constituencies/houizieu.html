Member of parliament

Houizieu is represented by Ms Caroline Weber (mps_party) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Weber was elected to represent Houizieu, with 28.9% of the valid votes. This was 0.599999999999998% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
