--- title: Carway subtitle: Carway is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Carway is represented by Mr Johann Vos (Green) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Vos was elected to represent Carway, with 32.5% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
Green 22,122 32.5%
Liberal 16,591 24.4%
Conservative 15,429 22.7%
Labour 13,886 20.4%
