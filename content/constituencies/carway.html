--- title: Carway subtitle: Carway is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Carway is represented by Mr Johann Vos (Green) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Vos was elected to represent Carway, with 28.6% of the valid votes. This was 0.900000000000002% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
