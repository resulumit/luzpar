--- title: Carway subtitle: Carway is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Carway is represented by Mr Johann Vos (mps_party) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Vos was elected to represent Carway, with 29.4% of the valid votes. This was 4.4% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
