--- title: Amdeck subtitle: Amdeck is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Amdeck is represented by Mr Lucas Frei (mps_party) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Frei was elected to represent Amdeck, with 28% of the valid votes. This was 1.7% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
