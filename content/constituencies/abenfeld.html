--- title: Abenfeld subtitle: Abenfeld is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Abenfeld is represented by Mr Ambre Meier (Liberal) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Meier was elected to represent Abenfeld, with 30.1% of the valid votes. This was 3.9% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
