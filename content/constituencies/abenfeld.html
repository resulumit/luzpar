--- title: Abenfeld subtitle: Abenfeld is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Abenfeld is represented by Mr Ambre Meier (Liberal) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Meier was elected to represent Abenfeld, with 34.5% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
