--- title: Kaisermold subtitle: Kaisermold is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Kaisermold is represented by Dr Julie Morel (Conservative) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Morel was elected to represent Kaisermold, with 28.6% of the valid votes. This was 0.900000000000002% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
