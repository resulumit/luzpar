--- title: Kaisermold subtitle: Kaisermold is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Kaisermold is represented by Dr Julie Morel (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Morel was elected to represent Kaisermold, with 30.7% of the valid votes. This was 5.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
