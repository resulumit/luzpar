--- title: Kaisermold subtitle: Kaisermold is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Kaisermold is represented by Dr Julie Morel (Conservative) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Morel was elected to represent Kaisermold, with 28.3% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
