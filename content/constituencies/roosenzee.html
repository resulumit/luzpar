--- title: Roosenzee subtitle: Roosenzee is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Roosenzee is represented by Mr Gerhard Smit (Conservative) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Smit was elected to represent Roosenzee, with 31.3% of the valid votes. This was 6.6% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
