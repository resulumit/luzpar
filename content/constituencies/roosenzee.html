--- title: Roosenzee subtitle: Roosenzee is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Roosenzee is represented by Mr Gerhard Smit (Conservative) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Smit was elected to represent Roosenzee, with 31.1% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
