--- title: Romkastel subtitle: Romkastel is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Romkastel is represented by Mr Samuel Mulder (Green) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Mulder was elected to represent Romkastel, with 29.4% of the valid votes. This was 1.8% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
