Member of parliament

Romkastel is represented by Mr Samuel Mulder (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Mulder was elected to represent Romkastel, with 29.3% of the valid votes. This was 0.600000000000001% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
