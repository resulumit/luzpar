--- title: Lieszona subtitle: Lieszona is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Lieszona is represented by Mr Josef Dubois (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Dubois was elected to represent Lieszona, with 32.1% of the valid votes. This was 5.4% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
