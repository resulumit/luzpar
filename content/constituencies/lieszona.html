--- title: Lieszona subtitle: Lieszona is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Lieszona is represented by Mr Josef Dubois (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Dubois was elected to represent Lieszona, with 31.5% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
