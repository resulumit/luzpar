--- title: Menkkon subtitle: Menkkon is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Menkkon is represented by Dr Lia Schmid (Independent) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Schmid was elected to represent Menkkon, with 31.4% of the valid votes. This was 3.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
