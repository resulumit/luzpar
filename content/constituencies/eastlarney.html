--- title: Eastlarney subtitle: Eastlarney is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Eastlarney is represented by Mrs Gabriel Galloway (Green) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Galloway was elected to represent Eastlarney, with 31.5% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
