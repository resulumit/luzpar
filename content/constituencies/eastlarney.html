--- title: Eastlarney subtitle: Eastlarney is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Eastlarney is represented by Mrs Gabriel Galloway (mps_party) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mrs Galloway was elected to represent Eastlarney, with 32.3% of the valid votes. This was 5.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
