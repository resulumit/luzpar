--- title: Diessenzona subtitle: Diessenzona is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Diessenzona is represented by Dr Marijke Neumann (Conservative) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Neumann was elected to represent Diessenzona, with 27.2% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
Conservative 36,006 27.2%
