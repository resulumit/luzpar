--- title: Diessenzona subtitle: Diessenzona is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Diessenzona is represented by Dr Marijke Neumann (mps_party) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Neumann was elected to represent Diessenzona, with 29.2% of the valid votes. This was 2.9% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
