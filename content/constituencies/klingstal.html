--- title: Klingstal subtitle: Klingstal is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Klingstal is represented by Dr Niels Richard (Green) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Richard was elected to represent Klingstal, with 32.8% of the valid votes. This was 7.6% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
