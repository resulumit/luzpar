--- title: Knokbergen subtitle: Knokbergen is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Knokbergen is represented by Dr Sanne Weber (mps_party) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Weber was elected to represent Knokbergen, with 31.7% of the valid votes. This was 7.3% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
