--- title: Épiluçon subtitle: Épiluçon is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Épiluçon is represented by Mr Louis de Jong (Liberal) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr De Jong was elected to represent Épiluçon, with 33.7% of the valid votes. This was 8.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
