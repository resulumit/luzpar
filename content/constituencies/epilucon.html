--- title: Épiluçon subtitle: Épiluçon is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Épiluçon is represented by Mr Louis de Jong (Liberal) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr De Jong was elected to represent Épiluçon, with 35.7% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
