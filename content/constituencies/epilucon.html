--- title: Épiluçon subtitle: Épiluçon is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Épiluçon is represented by Mr Louis de Jong (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr De Jong was elected to represent Épiluçon, with 30.9% of the valid votes. This was 3.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
