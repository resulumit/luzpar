--- title: Tangerhöring subtitle: Tangerhöring is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Tangerhöring is represented by Dr Michael Veenstra (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Veenstra was elected to represent Tangerhöring, with 28.7% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
