--- title: Tangerhöring subtitle: Tangerhöring is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Tangerhöring is represented by Dr Michael Veenstra (Liberal) since 2005.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Veenstra was elected to represent Tangerhöring, with 31.9% of the valid votes. This was 5.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
