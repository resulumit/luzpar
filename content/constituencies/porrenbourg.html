--- title: Porrenbourg subtitle: Porrenbourg is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Porrenbourg is represented by Dr Gabriel van Leeuwen (Green) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Van Leeuwen was elected to represent Porrenbourg, with 31.7% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
