--- title: Porrenbourg subtitle: Porrenbourg is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Porrenbourg is represented by Dr Gabriel van Leeuwen (Green) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Van Leeuwen was elected to represent Porrenbourg, with 32.2% of the valid votes. This was 6.4% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
