--- title: Loughnasloe subtitle: Loughnasloe is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Loughnasloe is represented by Ms Lara Groen (mps_party) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Groen was elected to represent Loughnasloe, with 30.1% of the valid votes. This was 1.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
