--- title: Loughnasloe subtitle: Loughnasloe is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Loughnasloe is represented by Ms Lara Groen (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Groen was elected to represent Loughnasloe, with 31.4% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
