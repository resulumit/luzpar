--- title: Loughnasloe subtitle: Loughnasloe is a parliamentary constituency in South Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Loughnasloe is represented by Ms Lara Groen (Liberal) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Groen was elected to represent Loughnasloe, with 30.6% of the valid votes. This was 4% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
