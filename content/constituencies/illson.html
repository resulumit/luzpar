--- title: Illson subtitle: Illson is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Illson is represented by Mr Ben Koch (Liberal) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Koch was elected to represent Illson, with 33% of the valid votes. This was 5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
