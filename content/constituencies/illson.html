Member of parliament

Illson is represented by Mr Ben Koch (mps_party) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Koch was elected to represent Illson, with 28.6% of the valid votes. This was 0.800000000000001% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
