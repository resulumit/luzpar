--- title: Illson subtitle: Illson is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Illson is represented by Mr Ben Koch (Liberal) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Koch was elected to represent Illson, with 31% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.

Party Votes Share
Liberal 28,682 31%
