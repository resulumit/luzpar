Member of parliament

Illson is represented by Mr Ben Koch (mps_party) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Koch was elected to represent Illson, with 28.2% of the valid votes. This was 0.599999999999998% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
