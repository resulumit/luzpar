--- title: Molenaarde subtitle: Molenaarde is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Molenaarde is represented by Ms Mila Wagner (Liberal) since 2010.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Ms Wagner was elected to represent Molenaarde, with 30.4% of the valid votes. This was 0.899999999999999% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
