--- title: Gordola subtitle: Gordola is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Gordola is represented by Dr Elias Fischer (mps_party) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Fischer was elected to represent Gordola, with 35.5% of the valid votes. This was 8.9% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
