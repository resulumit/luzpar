--- title: Gordola subtitle: Gordola is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Gordola is represented by Dr Elias Fischer (Labour) since 2020.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Fischer was elected to represent Gordola, with 27% of the valid votes.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
