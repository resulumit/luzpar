--- title: Gordola subtitle: Gordola is a parliamentary constituency in North Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Gordola is represented by Dr Elias Fischer (Labour) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Fischer was elected to represent Gordola, with 27.2% of the valid votes. This was 0.599999999999998% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
