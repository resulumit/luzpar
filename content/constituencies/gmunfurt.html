--- title: Gmunfurt subtitle: Gmunfurt is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Gmunfurt is represented by Dr Christine Hendriks (Green) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Hendriks was elected to represent Gmunfurt, with 30% of the valid votes. This was 4.5% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
