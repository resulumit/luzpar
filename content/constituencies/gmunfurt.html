--- title: Gmunfurt subtitle: Gmunfurt is a parliamentary constituency in West Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Gmunfurt is represented by Dr Christine Hendriks (Green) since 2015.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Dr Hendriks was elected to represent Gmunfurt, with 31.8% of the valid votes. This was 5.7% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.

The detailed results are presented in the table below.
