--- title: Benwerder subtitle: Benwerder is a parliamentary constituency in East Luzland. ---

Member of parliament

Benwerder is represented by Mr Chris Antony (Labour) since 2000.

Last election

In the 2020 Luzland general election, Mr Antony was elected to represent Benwerder, with 32.5% of the valid votes. This was 7.1% more than the votes cast for the second placed candidate in the constituency.
